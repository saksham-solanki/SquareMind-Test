## Why NRIs Are Investing in Ahmedabad in 2026

Ahmedabad offers some of the lowest stamp duty in India (4.9%), a booming business environment (Gujarat's industrial growth), and GIFT City — India's first operational smart city and international financial services centre. NRIs from the large Gujarati diaspora are particularly active in Ahmedabad real estate.

## Gujarat Stamp Duty and Registration

Gujarat charges 4.9% stamp duty — among the lowest in India. Registration charge is 1% additional. No significant gender-based concession. Total acquisition cost is approximately 6% of property value.

## Top Builders in Ahmedabad

Adani Realty, Goyal Group, Savvy Infrastructure, and Sun Builders. Verify:

1. Gujarat RERA registration on gujrera.gujarat.gov.in
2. Builder track record in Ahmedabad
3. NRI documentation and POA support
4. NRI home loan bank partnerships

## FEMA Compliance

Standard [FEMA rules](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Ahmedabad-specific:

- GIFT City properties may have special IFSC regulations — verify applicable framework
- Agricultural land (common in Ahmedabad outskirts) requires NA conversion
- POA must be Consulate-attested and registered at Gujarat Sub-Registrar

## NRI Tax Implications

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax liability.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS. GIFT City tenants provide premium 3.5-4.5% yields
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG without indexation. 20% TDS on sale
- **GIFT City special rules:** IFSC units may have tax benefits — consult CA for specific GIFT City investments

## Property Verification

1. **Title search** from independent Ahmedabad lawyer
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from Gujarat Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **Gujarat RERA registration** verification
4. **NA (Non-Agricultural) conversion** for plots outside AMC limits
5. **TP scheme** (Town Planning Scheme) status for the locality
