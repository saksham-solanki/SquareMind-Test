## Ahmedabad Property Verification

Ahmedabad has relatively straightforward property documentation. Gujarat RERA is active, and land records are increasingly digitised.

## Step 1: Gujarat RERA Verification

Check gujrera.gujarat.gov.in for project registration. Verify all project details.

Use our [RERA Verification Tool](/tools/rera-verifier) for guided verification.

## Step 2: Land Records

**For rural/suburban land:**
- 7/12 extract (Saat-Baara) from Taluka office
- Village Form No. 6 (Hak Patrak) — ownership rights record
- Online at anyror.gujarat.gov.in

**For urban properties:**
- City survey records from AMC (Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation)
- Property card / milkat patrak

## Step 3: NA Permission

Non-Agricultural permission is required for residential development on agricultural land. Common in SG Highway outskirts, South Bopal, and Shela areas.

- Verify NA order from the District Collector
- Check if NA premium has been paid
- Ensure final order (not provisional)

## Step 4: AMC/AUDA Approvals

| Authority | Jurisdiction |
| --- | --- |
| AMC (Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation) | Core city |
| AUDA (Ahmedabad Urban Dev Authority) | Extended city |
| GIFT City Authority | GIFT City area |

Building permission, development permission, and completion certificate from relevant authority.

## Red Flags to Watch For

1. **NA not obtained** — particularly in SG Highway extension and Shela outskirts
2. **TP (Town Planning) scheme pending** — land may be reserved or affected by road widening
3. **Revenue records mismatch** — seller name not matching 7/12 or village records
4. **GIFT City land** — separate regulatory framework; verify with GIFT City Authority specifically
5. **Waqf Board claims** — relevant in older parts of Ahmedabad

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I check 7/12 extract online in Gujarat?

Visit anyror.gujarat.gov.in. Select district (Ahmedabad), taluka, and village. Enter survey number to view ownership and land classification records.

### Is GIFT City property subject to Gujarat RERA?

GIFT City has its own regulatory authority (IFSCA). However, residential projects within GIFT City are also registered under Gujarat RERA. Verify with both authorities.
