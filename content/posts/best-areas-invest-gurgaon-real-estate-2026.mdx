## Gurgaon: NCR's Corporate Capital and Real Estate Engine

Gurgaon (Gurugram) has evolved from a Haryana district into India's corporate capital. DLF Cyber City, Udyog Vihar, Golf Course Road, and the Dwarka Expressway corridor house Fortune 500 headquarters, creating 15 lakh+ corporate jobs and the deepest rental demand in NCR.

The city's real estate market in 2026 is driven by two forces: Dwarka Expressway completion (the decade's biggest infrastructure event for Gurgaon) and the ongoing shift of corporate offices from Delhi to Gurgaon.

## Dwarka Expressway: The Star Performer

The 29 km Dwarka Expressway connecting Dwarka (Delhi) to NH-8 (Gurgaon) has delivered transformative price appreciation. Sectors 103, 106, 108, 109, 111, and 113 along the expressway have seen 40-60% price increases over three years.

**Key projects:** DLF The Arbour (Sec 63), Godrej Meridien (Sec 106), Sobha City (Sec 108), Tata Primanti (Sec 72).

**Current assessment:** At ₹8,000-14,000/sqft, the expressway is no longer the bargain it was in 2022. We rate it A- for new entry — strong fundamentals but much of the infrastructure premium is already priced in.

### New Gurgaon: The Balanced Choice

Sectors 76-95 in New Gurgaon offer the best balance of price (₹6,500-10,000/sqft), employment proximity (15-20 min to Cyber City), and yield (2.8-3.4%). Godrej, Shapoorji Pallonji, and Emaar have quality projects.

**Best for:** Investors seeking both appreciation and rental income without paying Dwarka Expressway or Golf Course Extension premiums.

## Sohna Road: The Emerging Corridor

Sohna Road benefits from proximity to Southern Peripheral Road (SPR) and the upcoming Delhi-Mumbai Expressway. At ₹5,500-8,000/sqft, it's 30-40% below Dwarka Expressway. Central Park, BPTP, and Emaar have projects here.

**Risk:** Infrastructure gaps — water supply and last-mile road quality remain below Dwarka Expressway standards.

## The Bottom Line

Gurgaon in 2026 remains NCR's best real estate market — deep corporate employment, improving infrastructure, and India's strongest developer ecosystem (DLF, Godrej, Sobha all active). New Gurgaon offers the best entry point for balanced returns. Dwarka Expressway is maturing but still sound. Avoid Golf Course Road at ₹15,000+/sqft unless buying for end-use.
