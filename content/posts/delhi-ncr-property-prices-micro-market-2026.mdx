## Investment Insight

Dwarka Expressway (Gurgaon) and Yamuna Expressway (Noida) are NCR's growth corridors. However, NCR carries higher builder risk than other metros. Verify builder credentials carefully.

**Warning:** Greater Noida West has seen multiple project abandonments. Only buy from listed companies or conglomerate-backed developers.
