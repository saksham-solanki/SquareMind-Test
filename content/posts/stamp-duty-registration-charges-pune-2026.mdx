## Pune Stamp Duty Rates (2026)

Pune follows Maharashtra Stamp Act rates:

| Category | PMC Limits | PCMC Limits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Male buyer | 6% | 6% |
| Female buyer (sole/first-named) | 5% | 5% |
| Registration charge | 1% (max Rs 30,000) | 1% (max Rs 30,000) |
| Metro cess | 1% | 1% |
| **Total (male)** | **8%** | **8%** |
| **Total (female)** | **7%** | **7%** |

## PMC vs PCMC: Key Differences

| Factor | PMC (Pune Municipal Corporation) | PCMC (Pimpri Chinchwad) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Stamp duty | Same | Same |
| Ready Reckoner values | Higher | 15-30% lower |
| Property tax rates | Higher | Lower |
| Metro cess | Yes | Yes |
| Key areas | Baner, Kothrud, Hinjewadi | Wakad, Pimpri, Chinchwad |

## How to Pay Stamp Duty in Pune

1. Visit igrmaharashtra.gov.in
2. Select Pune district and Sub-Registrar office
3. Enter property details
4. System calculates stamp duty based on Ready Reckoner
5. Pay online via net banking / NEFT
6. Book SRO appointment for registration

## Cost-Saving Tips

1. Register in woman's name to save 1% (Rs 50K-1L on typical Pune property)
2. Verify Ready Reckoner value before negotiating — some areas have inflated RR
3. Consider PCMC areas for lower overall acquisition cost
4. Check for metro cess applicability based on property location
