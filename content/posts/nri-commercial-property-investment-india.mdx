## Why NRIs Should Consider Commercial Property

Commercial property in India offers 6-9% rental yields compared to 2-4% for residential — significantly better cash flow. With FEMA permitting NRI commercial property purchase without RBI approval, it is an attractive option for income-focused investors.

## Commercial vs Residential for NRIs

| Factor | Commercial | Residential |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rental yield | 6-9% | 2-4% |
| Lease tenure | 3-9 years (locked) | 11 months (standard) |
| Tenant quality | Companies (reliable) | Individuals (variable) |
| Vacancy risk | Higher (longer to fill) | Lower (faster to fill) |
| Entry price | Higher | Lower |
| Appreciation | Moderate | Higher |
| GST on rent | 18% (if tenant is GST-registered) | None |

### Retail Space

Pre-leased retail spaces in established malls or high-street locations offer 7-10% yields with long-term brand tenants.

## FEMA Rules for Commercial Property

Same rules as residential: NRE/NRO payment channels, no cash transactions, POA for remote execution. No additional RBI approval needed for commercial property purchase.

## Tax Treatment

- **Rental income:** Taxed under "Income from Other Sources" (not House Property). 30% TDS by tenant
- **GST:** 18% on commercial rent (if tenant is GST-registered). Landlord must register for GST
- **Capital gains:** Same LTCG/STCG rules as residential
- **Section 54F:** When selling commercial property, reinvest full sale proceeds in residential property for LTCG exemption

## REIT Alternative for NRIs

If direct commercial property ownership seems complex, consider Indian REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts):

- Embassy Office Parks REIT
- Mindspace Business Parks REIT
- Brookfield India Real Estate Trust

REITs offer: lower entry point, professional management, liquidity (listed on stock exchange), and 6-8% distribution yield.
