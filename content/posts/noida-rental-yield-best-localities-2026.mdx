## Noida Rental Yields: Best Value in NCR

Noida consistently delivers NCR's highest rental yields because capital values are 40-50% below Gurgaon while rental demand from IT corridor employment is proportionally strong. The math is straightforward: lower purchase price + decent rent = better yield.

## Top Yield Picks

### Greater Noida West: NCR's Yield Leader

Greater Noida West delivers NCR's highest yields at the lowest capital outlay. A 2BHK costing ₹25-38L rents for ₹9,000-14,000/month. Tenants include IT professionals from Noida SEZs, BPO employees, and university students/staff.

**Critical caveat:** Only buy delivered, occupied buildings. Verify occupancy rate (70%+ is acceptable) before investing — low-occupancy buildings have higher vacancy risk.

### Knowledge Park: Student + IT Demand

Knowledge Park in Greater Noida benefits from dual demand — Amity University, Bennett University, and Galgotia campus create student housing demand, while IT companies in the park create professional demand. Yields of 3.2-3.8% are consistent.

## PG Opportunity

Noida's university corridor creates strong PG demand. A 3BHK in Greater Noida or Knowledge Park operated as PG can generate ₹30,000-40,000/month vs ₹12,000-16,000 traditional rent. The student demographic means higher turnover but nearly zero vacancy during academic sessions.

## Risk Factors

- **Maintenance defaults:** Some builder societies in Greater Noida have poor maintenance management — inspect before buying
- **Builder disputes:** Ongoing legal disputes between buyers and builders can affect resale
- **Infrastructure gaps:** Some Greater Noida West sectors lack adequate road and water infrastructure

## The Bottom Line

Noida offers NCR's best rental yields — 3.0-4.0% gross at entry prices that are half of Gurgaon. Greater Noida West and Knowledge Park lead for yield. The trade-off: more due diligence required and potentially lower liquidity. If you can manage the extra verification work, the returns justify it.
