## What the Data Tells Us

**Healthy supply-demand balance (< 15 months inventory):** Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad — prices will continue rising.

**Moderate oversupply (15-20 months):** Mumbai, Chennai, Ahmedabad — prices stable to moderately rising.

**Oversupply concern (> 20 months):** Delhi NCR, Kolkata — price appreciation will be slower.

## Supply-Side Price Pressure

| Scenario | Months of Inventory | Expected Price Impact |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Undersupply | < 10 months | +10-15% annual appreciation |
| Balanced | 10-15 months | +6-10% annual appreciation |
| Moderate oversupply | 15-20 months | +3-6% annual appreciation |
| Significant oversupply | > 20 months | 0-3% or price correction |

## Investment Implication

**Buy in tight supply markets** (Hyderabad, Bangalore) for appreciation. **Avoid oversupplied corridors** in NCR and Kolkata unless buying at distressed prices.
