## The Vastu Dilemma for First-Time Buyers

Many Indian homebuyers feel pressured to comply with Vastu Shastra principles. Some reject properties solely because the "main door faces south." This guide separates Vastu principles that have practical merit from those that cost you good properties.

## Vastu Principles with Practical Basis

| Vastu Principle | Practical Reasoning | Follow? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| North/East-facing entrance | More morning sunlight | Nice to have |
| Kitchen in southeast | Maximum sunlight for hygiene | Somewhat valid |
| Bedroom in southwest | Cooler in summer (less afternoon sun) | Minor benefit |
| Bathroom away from kitchen | Hygiene and plumbing separation | Good design practice |
| No beam above bed | Psychological comfort | Structural, not Vastu |
| Cross-ventilation | Airflow for comfort | Excellent design principle |

## Vastu Principles That May Cost You a Better Property

| Common Vastu "Rule" | Reality Check |
| --- | --- |
| "South-facing is bad" | No impact on living quality. South-facing gets good sunlight. |
| "Door must face exactly East" | Orientation has negligible impact on modern apartment living |
| "Staircase must not be in center" | Structural design decision, not quality indicator |
| "No mirror opposite bed" | Personal preference, not property quality |
| "Water tank only in West" | Plumbing design, not quality indicator |

## Vastu and Resale Value: The Real Impact

Here is where Vastu matters regardless of your personal beliefs: **resale liquidity.**

- North/East-facing flats typically sell 5-10% faster
- "Vastu-compliant" is a marketing advantage in Indian market
- Some buyers will reject otherwise perfect properties for Vastu
- This is a market reality, not a recommendation

## The Balanced Approach

1. **Prioritize:** Location, price, construction quality, carpet area, ventilation, natural light
2. **Consider:** Direction-facing as a tiebreaker between similar properties
3. **Do not:** Reject a significantly better property solely for Vastu non-compliance
4. **Do not:** Pay a premium more than 5% for "Vastu compliance"

## What Actually Affects Living Quality

1. Cross-ventilation (windows on opposite walls)
2. Natural light (sunlight hours per day)
3. Noise level (road-facing vs garden-facing)
4. Water pressure and plumbing quality
5. Floor level and elevator access
6. Parking convenience
7. Society maintenance quality
