## 150 Years of Building

Shapoorji Pallonji Group has been constructing for over 150 years — they built the Reserve Bank of India building, the Oberoi Hotel, and the original Bombay Stock Exchange. Their real estate arm, however, is relatively newer.

## Company Overview

- **Private company** (part of Shapoorji Pallonji Group)
- **Headquarters:** Mumbai
- **Founded:** 1865 (group); real estate arm active since 2008
- **Active cities:** Mumbai, Pune
- **Brands:** Joyville (mid-segment), Shapoorji Pallonji (premium)
- **Revenue (FY2025):** ~₹2,100 crore (estimated — unlisted)

## RERA Delivery Track Record

Analysis of 14 Shapoorji Pallonji residential projects under MahaRERA (2017-2023).

| Category | Projects | % of Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Delivered on time | 6 | 42.9% |
| Delayed 6–18 months | 5 | 35.7% |
| Delayed 18–36 months | 2 | 14.3% |
| Significantly delayed | 1 | 7.1% |

**Assessment:** 42.9% on-time delivery on a smaller portfolio. Construction quality reflects their infrastructure background. The one significantly delayed project has land title complications unrelated to execution capability.

## Financial Transparency Concern

As an unlisted, private group, Shapoorji Pallonji does not file publicly audited real estate financials. This is a meaningful transparency gap compared to listed peers. The group has faced well-publicised liquidity challenges, though the real estate arm appears ring-fenced.

**Risk factor:** Without public financial disclosure, buyers have less visibility into the developer's financial health.

## Construction Quality

Shapoorji Pallonji's construction DNA is genuine. Coming from an infrastructure background (bridges, dams, commercial buildings), their structural quality is typically above average.

- **Structure:** Reinforced concrete with higher-than-standard specifications
- **Finishes:** Premium, especially in their branded premium line
- **Joyville (mid-segment):** Good value proposition with above-average build quality for the price point

## Our Verdict

**Overall Grade: B**

Excellent construction quality backed by 150 years of building heritage. The main concern is financial transparency — as an unlisted company, buyers cannot independently verify financial health. Stick to Mumbai and Pune where they have established presence.
