## Why Remote Verification Is Critical for NRIs

NRI property fraud is a significant problem in India. Without physical presence, NRIs are vulnerable to forged documents, encumbered titles, unapproved constructions, and inflated valuations. Here is how to verify documents without visiting India.

## Documents You Can Verify Online

### 1. RERA Registration

Every state has an online RERA portal where you can verify project registration:

| State | Portal | What to Check |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Maharashtra | maharera.mahaonline.gov.in | Project ID, completion date, promoter details |
| Karnataka | rera.karnataka.gov.in | Registration number, project status |
| Telangana | telangana.rera.gov.in | Project details, complaints |
| Tamil Nadu | tnrera.in | Registration status |
| UP | up-rera.in | Project and agent registration |
| Haryana | haryanarera.gov.in | Project status, builder details |

### 2. Encumbrance Certificate

Most states now offer online EC verification:
- Telangana: registration.telangana.gov.in
- Karnataka: kaveri.karnataka.gov.in
- Tamil Nadu: tnreginet.gov.in

### 3. Property Tax Records

Municipal corporation websites show property tax payment history, which confirms ownership and property existence.

### 4. Circle Rate / Ready Reckoner Value

State registration department websites publish area-wise circle rates. Compare with the quoted price to identify overvaluation.

## Documents Requiring Lawyer Verification

Some documents cannot be fully verified online. Engage an independent property lawyer in the city (not recommended by the builder or broker):

1. **Title chain** — 30-year history of ownership transfers
2. **Mutation records** — revenue department land records
3. **Approved building plan** — from municipal corporation
4. **NOC from planning authority** (DTCP, CMDA, HMDA, etc.)
5. **Occupation Certificate** — for ready-to-move properties

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Builder refuses to share RERA registration number
- "Special NRI price" significantly above market rate
- Pressure to pay booking amount before document review
- POA requested in favor of broker
- Property in unauthorized colony or unapproved layout

## How to Engage a Remote Lawyer

1. Use bar association directories for the city
2. Verify lawyer's enrollment number on Bar Council website
3. Pay a fixed fee (Rs 15,000-30,000) for complete title search and report
4. Request video call walkthrough of findings
5. Get written legal opinion on title status
