## Bangalore's Growth Has Split Into Two Corridors

North Bangalore (Devanahalli, Hebbal, Yelahanka, Thanisandra) and South Bangalore (Electronic City, Kanakapura Road, JP Nagar, Bannerghatta Road) offer distinct investment propositions in 2026.

## Corridor Comparison

| Parameter | North Bangalore | South Bangalore | Advantage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Avg Rate (₹/sqft) | 5,500–9,000 | 6,000–10,000 | North |
| 3-Year CAGR | 14–20% | 8–14% | North |
| Rental Yield | 2.5–3.5% | 3.0–4.2% | South |
| Airport Proximity | 15–30 min | 60–90 min | North |
| IT Employment | Manyata Tech Park | Electronic City, Bommasandra | South |
| Social Infrastructure | Developing | Established | South |

## North Bangalore: The Growth Story

The Kempegowda International Airport has catalyzed North Bangalore's transformation. Devanahalli, once a distant village, now hosts aerospace SEZ, IT parks, and luxury residential projects by Prestige, Brigade, and Godrej.

**Key catalysts:** Airport expansion, Aerospace SEZ, BIAL IT Investment Region, and proposed metro extension to airport.

The [Bangalore rental analysis](/insights/bangalore-rental-market-yields-2026) shows North Bangalore's yields improving as social infrastructure catches up.

## South Bangalore: The Yield Champion

Electronic City (Phase 1 and 2) hosts Infosys, Wipro, and TCS campuses with 200,000+ IT employees. This captive demand ensures high rental occupancy rates (95%+) and strong yields.

**Established advantage:** Schools, hospitals, malls, and social infrastructure are already in place. No waiting for development — tenants move in immediately.

## Recommendation

**Capital growth seekers:** North Bangalore (Devanahalli, Thanisandra) with a 5+ year horizon.
**Rental income seekers:** South Bangalore (Electronic City, JP Nagar) for immediate yield.

## FAQs

### Is North Bangalore too far from the city center?

Currently yes — but metro extension and highway improvements are rapidly reducing travel times. By 2028, airport area will feel much more connected.

### Which corridor has better builders?

Both — Prestige, Brigade, Sobha, and Puravankara are active in both corridors. Focus on project-specific quality, not corridor-level builder presence.

### Can I invest in both corridors?

Ideal strategy — one North Bangalore property (growth) and one South Bangalore property (yield) creates a balanced Bangalore portfolio.
