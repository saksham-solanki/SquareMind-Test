## Fractional Ownership: Access Premium Real Estate from ₹10 Lakhs

Fractional real estate ownership allows investors to buy a share of a commercial property — typically a Grade A office space — for ₹10-25 lakhs instead of ₹1-3 crore for full ownership. Platforms like Strata, hBits, PropertyShare, and Fracxn manage the properties, collect rent, and distribute income proportionally.

## How It Works

| Step | Process |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Browse | Choose from curated commercial properties on the platform |
| 2. Invest | Invest ₹10-25L for a proportional share (5-25% of property) |
| 3. Earn | Receive monthly/quarterly rental income (6-9% pre-tax yield) |
| 4. Exit | Sell your share on the platform's secondary market (3-7 year typical hold) |
| 5. Appreciate | Share in capital appreciation when the property is sold |

## Returns Comparison

| Investment Type | Min Investment | Yield | Appreciation | Total Return | Liquidity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fractional ownership | ₹10-25L | 6-9% | 4-8% | 10-17% | LOW (secondary market) |
| REITs | ₹15,000 | 6-7% | 3-5% | 9-12% | HIGH (exchange-traded) |
| Direct commercial | ₹80L-2Cr | 6-9% | 5-10% | 11-19% | MEDIUM |
| Direct residential | ₹25-50L | 2-4% | 8-16% | 10-20% | MEDIUM-HIGH |

## Advantages Over Direct Ownership

- **Lower entry point:** ₹10L vs ₹80L+ for direct commercial
- **Professional management:** No tenant hassles, maintenance, or vacancy management
- **Diversification:** Spread ₹30L across 3 properties in 3 cities vs one property in one city
- **Grade A access:** Invest in premium office spaces (Mindspace, Embassy) typically unavailable to individual investors

## Risks and Limitations

1. **Regulatory uncertainty:** SEBI issued guidelines for Small and Medium REITs (SM REITs) in 2024, but the regulatory framework is still evolving
2. **Liquidity risk:** Secondary market for fractional shares is thin — selling may take 3-6 months
3. **Platform risk:** If the platform shuts down, property management continuity is uncertain
4. **Concentration risk:** Most properties are Grade A office in Bangalore, Mumbai, or Hyderabad — limited geographic diversity
5. **Fee structure:** Platforms charge 1-2% management fees + exit fees that reduce net returns

## Our Assessment

Fractional ownership is a legitimate addition to an investor's toolkit, particularly for:
- Investors with ₹10-25L who want commercial exposure
- Those who want diversification across property types
- Passive investors who don't want management responsibility

**Caution:** Don't treat it as a replacement for direct investment. The illiquidity premium is real — your money is locked up for 3-7 years. Only invest money you won't need during that period.

## The Bottom Line

Fractional ownership democratises commercial real estate access. Returns of 10-17% are competitive, but illiquidity and regulatory uncertainty demand caution. Allocate 10-20% of your real estate budget to fractional, not 100%.
