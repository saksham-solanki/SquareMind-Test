## What to Verify Before Buying

Regardless of builder grade, always verify: specific project RERA registration, SPV name and complaint history, actual construction progress, occupation certificate status for ready properties.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is the most reliable builder in Mumbai?

Based on data, Oberoi Realty has the best delivery track record (62.5% on-time) and strongest financials among Mumbai builders. For safety, Tata Housing offers the lowest project completion risk.

### Which Mumbai builder offers best value for money?

Lodha's Palava Township and Shapoorji Pallonji's Joyville brand offer the best value in terms of amenities and quality per rupee spent. Premium-segment buyers get best value from Godrej in Vikhroli.
