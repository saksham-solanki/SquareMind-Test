## Gurgaon 2026: The Post-Infrastructure Playbook

Dwarka Expressway delivered the biggest price jump in Gurgaon's history. What's next? Smart money in Gurgaon is now focusing on three themes: corporate HQ migration from Delhi, New Gurgaon value capture, and commercial real estate yield.

## Top Strategies

### Strategy 1: New Gurgaon Value Corridor

As Dwarka Expressway prices exceed ₹10,000/sqft, New Gurgaon (Sectors 76-95) at ₹6,500-10,000/sqft is where value investors are moving. NH-8 corporate expansion and metro connectivity improvements will drive next-phase appreciation.

**Best picks:** Godrej Meridien (Sec 106), Shapoorji Pallonji Joyville (Sec 102) — national brands at competitive pricing.

### Strategy 2: DLF Capital Preservation

DLF projects are Gurgaon's equivalent of blue-chip stocks. Privana West (Sec 76) at ₹14,000-18,000 offers near-zero default risk, 15-20% resale premium, and consistent 8-10% annual appreciation. Smart money parks capital here when other corridors seem risky.

### Strategy 3: Sohna Road Emerging Bet

Sohna Road at ₹5,500-8,000/sqft benefits from SPR connectivity and Delhi-Mumbai Expressway. EMAAR, Central Park, and BPTP have projects. Higher risk than New Gurgaon but 20-30% cheaper.
