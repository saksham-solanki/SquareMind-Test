## The Verdict: It Depends

**Tier-2 advantages:**
- Lower entry prices (₹3,500-5,500 vs ₹7,500-18,500/sqft)
- Infrastructure investment catching up (metro, highways, airports)
- Growing IT sector presence (Indore, Vizag, Coimbatore)
- Better rental yields in some markets

**Tier-2 disadvantages:**
- Lower liquidity — harder to sell quickly
- Limited institutional builder presence — higher builder risk
- Weaker RERA enforcement in most states
- Limited rental demand outside specific corridors
