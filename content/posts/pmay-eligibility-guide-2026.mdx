## What Is PMAY?

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is the government's flagship affordable housing scheme that provides interest subsidy on home loans for first-time buyers. The subsidy reduces your effective EMI significantly.

## Eligibility Criteria

| Category | Annual Income | Subsidy | On Loan Up To | For Tenure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| EWS | Up to Rs 3L | 6.5% | Rs 6L | 20 years |
| LIG | Rs 3L-6L | 6.5% | Rs 6L | 20 years |
| MIG-I | Rs 6L-12L | 4% | Rs 9L | 20 years |
| MIG-II | Rs 12L-18L | 3% | Rs 12L | 20 years |

### Key Eligibility Requirements

1. **First-time buyer:** You or your spouse must not own a pucca house anywhere in India
2. **Aadhaar mandatory:** For identification
3. **Income proof:** Salary slips, ITR, or Form 16
4. **Property carpet area limits:** EWS: up to 30 sq mt, LIG: up to 60 sq mt, MIG-I: up to 120 sq mt, MIG-II: up to 150 sq mt

## How Much Do You Save?

| Category | Subsidy Amount (NPV) | EMI Reduction (approx) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EWS/LIG | Rs 2.67L | Rs 2,200/month |
| MIG-I | Rs 2.35L | Rs 2,000/month |
| MIG-II | Rs 2.30L | Rs 1,900/month |

## Application Process

1. **Through your bank:** Most banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI) process PMAY applications along with home loan
2. **Online:** Visit pmaymis.gov.in for direct application
3. **Common Service Centre (CSC):** For in-person application

### Documents Required

- Aadhaar card (all family members)
- Income proof (salary slips / ITR)
- Property details (agreement, builder details)
- Bank account details
- Caste certificate (for reservation categories)
- Affidavit: do not own pucca house

## Check Application Status

Visit pmaymis.gov.in > Track Assessment > Enter Aadhaar or Application ID.

## Common Reasons for Rejection

1. Already own a pucca house (even if in another city)
2. Spouse owns a house (joint eligibility check)
3. Income exceeds category limit
4. Property carpet area exceeds category limit
5. Incomplete documentation
