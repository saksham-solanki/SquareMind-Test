## IT City Mohali: North India's Emerging Tech Corridor

IT City Mohali is transforming Chandigarh Tri-City from a government town into a tech-driven economy. With Infosys, TCS, Quark, and multiple startups establishing operations, the real estate dynamics are shifting from government salary-driven to IT-salary-driven demand.

## Why Chandigarh Tri-City Is Different

### Planned Infrastructure
Unlike most Indian cities, Chandigarh's grid-based sector system ensures:
- Orderly development without unauthorized colonies
- Predictable traffic patterns
- Consistent property quality standards
- Government-controlled land release (limiting oversupply)

### Livability Premium
Chandigarh consistently ranks as India's most livable city:
- Highest per-capita income among UTs
- Clean air and green cover
- Low crime rate
- Quality healthcare (PGI, PGIMER, Fortis, Max)
- Top educational institutions

### Supply Constraint
Unlike Hyderabad or Pune where supply is abundant, Chandigarh Tri-City has limited land release. This structural supply constraint supports price appreciation even with moderate demand growth.

**Medium-term (3-5 years):** Aerocity area for airport expansion catalyst. Buy before airport terminal expansion completes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Chandigarh better than Pune for IT investment?

Chandigarh offers lower entry costs and less competition from institutional investors. However, Pune has a larger IT ecosystem and better rental demand. For conservative investors seeking livability and steady appreciation, Chandigarh edges ahead.

### Which is better — Mohali or Zirakpur?

Mohali sectors near IT City offer better appreciation potential. Zirakpur offers lower entry prices and NH-22 connectivity to Chandigarh. For investment, Mohali IT corridor. For budget buying, Zirakpur.

### Is the Chandigarh real estate market liquid?

Moderate liquidity — better than most tier-2 cities but below Pune or Bangalore. Resale in IT corridor sectors (66-80) is relatively quick (2-4 months). Peripheral sectors may take 6-12 months.
