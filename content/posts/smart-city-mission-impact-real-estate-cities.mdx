## Smart City Mission: Real Impact or Just Branding?

The Smart City Mission covers 100 cities with a combined investment of Rs 2.05 lakh crore. But the real estate impact varies dramatically by city. Some have seen 15-25% appreciation from smart city projects. Others have seen minimal change.

## How Smart City Projects Affect Property Prices

### Direct Impact Zone (0-2km from project)
- Infrastructure improvement (roads, drainage, utilities) directly increases livability
- Property appreciation: 10-20% above city average
- Rental demand increase: 15-25%
- Timeline: 2-4 years from project start

### Indirect Impact Zone (2-5km)
- Spillover connectivity benefits
- Property appreciation: 5-10% above city average
- New commercial development attracted by improved infrastructure
- Timeline: 3-5 years

### Minimal Impact Zone (5km+)
- Marginal benefit from city-level improvements
- Property appreciation: in line with city average
- Primary benefit: city branding and perception improvement

## Investment Strategy for Smart City Areas

1. **Identify specific projects** — not all smart city funds are equal. Transport and road projects have the biggest real estate impact
2. **Buy in direct impact zone** — within 2km of smart city project site
3. **Verify project status** — many projects are delayed. Check progress on smartcities.gov.in
4. **Target pre-completion phase** — biggest appreciation happens during construction, not after

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Smart City label a reliable property investment signal?

No. The label alone means nothing for property prices. What matters is the specific projects being implemented and their proximity to your property. A Smart City with Rs 500 Cr in e-governance spending won't impact real estate like one building a Rs 2,000 Cr transit system.

### Will Smart City Mission Phase 2 happen?

The government has indicated continued investment in urban infrastructure. Whether formally branded as "Smart City 2.0" or rolled into other missions, the infrastructure development trajectory will continue.

### How do I check Smart City project progress?

Visit smartcities.gov.in for project-wise progress reports. Also check local municipal corporation websites for implementation updates. Ground-truth by visiting project sites.
