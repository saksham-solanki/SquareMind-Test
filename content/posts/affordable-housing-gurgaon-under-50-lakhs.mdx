## Under ₹50 Lakhs in Gurgaon: Limited but Possible

Gurgaon under ₹50 lakhs is genuinely challenging — this is India's most expensive NCR sub-market. But options exist in outer sectors and the Sohna Road corridor, primarily in 1BHK configurations or from mid-tier developers.

## Reality Check

At ₹50L in Gurgaon, your options are significantly more limited than in Noida (where ₹50L gets a 2BHK from a reputed builder) or Pune (where it gets a 2BHK in an IT corridor). If budget is constrained to ₹50L, consider whether Greater Noida West or Faridabad offers better value than trying to squeeze into Gurgaon.

### Our Pick: Sohna Town

If you're committed to Gurgaon, Sohna town (not Sohna Road in Gurgaon) offers the best ₹50L option — a 2BHK at ₹30-45L with improving connectivity via the KMP Expressway and growing employment from Gurgaon spillover.
