## Chandigarh Tri-City: The Tier-2 Alpha Generator

Smart money is increasingly looking beyond tier-1 metros for alpha. Chandigarh Tri-City offers a unique combination: highest per capita income among tier-2 cities, growing IT employment in Mohali, NRI diaspora demand from Canada/UK/Australia, and government-backed demand stability.

## Top Strategies

### Strategy 1: Mohali IT City Growth

IT City Mohali at ₹5,500-8,000/sqft is the primary growth corridor. Infosys, Quark, and the expanding tech ecosystem create a tenant base similar to a smaller version of Hinjewadi or Electronic City.

### Strategy 2: NRI Plotted Development

The Punjabi diaspora has strong emotional and financial ties to the Tri-City. Plotted developments in GMADA-approved sectors (New Chandigarh) at ₹4,000-6,000/sqft cater to NRI demand for land ownership. This creates a demand floor that other tier-2 cities lack.

### Strategy 3: Zirakpur Yield Play

Zirakpur at ₹4,500-6,500/sqft delivers 3.2-3.8% yield from government employees and Chandigarh commuters. The lowest capital outlay for decent yields in North India.
