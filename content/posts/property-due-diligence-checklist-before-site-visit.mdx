## Do This Research Before You Even Visit the Site

Most buyers do due diligence after falling in love with a property. By then, emotional commitment makes it hard to walk away. Flip the process: verify fundamentals first, visit only shortlisted projects.

## The 20-Point Pre-Visit Checklist

### RERA Verification (5 points)

| # | Check | How | Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | RERA registration exists | State RERA portal | No registration = walk away |
| 2 | Registered carpet area matches marketing | Compare RERA vs brochure | More than 3% difference |
| 3 | Completion deadline | RERA portal | Less than 12 months remaining |
| 4 | RERA complaints count | Portal complaint section | More than 20 per 100 units |
| 5 | Quarterly updates filed | Portal project updates | Missing for 2+ quarters |

### Builder Verification (5 points)

| # | Check | How | Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | Builder's other projects | RERA portal (search by promoter) | Multiple delayed projects |
| 7 | Delivery track record | Cross-reference RERA dates | Less than 60% on-time |
| 8 | Financial health (listed builders) | Annual report / BSE/NSE | Debt-to-equity above 2x |
| 9 | Legal cases | eCourts.gov.in search | Multiple buyer lawsuits |
| 10 | RERA penalties | RERA orders section | Any penalty orders |

### Legal Status (5 points)

| # | Check | How | Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 11 | Land title clarity | Revenue department records | Any litigation pending |
| 12 | Environmental clearance | MOEF portal | Project in CRZ/eco-sensitive zone |
| 13 | Approved building plan | Municipal authority | Plan different from marketing |
| 14 | Airport/defense no-objection | Height restrictions check | Near airport/military area |
| 15 | Encumbrance certificate | Sub-registrar office | Any charges or liens |

### Financial Assessment (5 points)

| # | Check | How | Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 | Price vs registrations nearby | State IGR portal | 20%+ above recent registrations |
| 17 | Bank approvals | Ask builder for list | Less than 3 banks approved |
| 18 | Payment plan structure | Builder offer document | More than 30% before foundation |
| 19 | Hidden charges | Ask for all-in cost sheet | Charges not listed in brochure |
| 20 | Resale liquidity | Property portals | Zero resale listings in area |

## The 2-Hour Research Process

All 20 checks can be completed online in approximately 2 hours:
- RERA portal: 30 minutes
- Builder background: 30 minutes
- Legal and title: 30 minutes
- Financial assessment: 30 minutes

This eliminates 60-70% of projects before you spend a weekend on site visits.

## What to Verify During the Actual Site Visit

After clearing the 20-point check:
- Physical construction progress vs RERA-reported progress
- Approach road and connectivity
- Neighboring land use (any future risks — factories, waste plants)
- Construction quality of completed units
- Water source and infrastructure
- Existing resident conversations (in phased projects)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How reliable is RERA portal data?

RERA data is filed by the builder and verified by the authority. It's the most reliable public source but not infallible. Cross-reference with physical verification and independent checks. Some state portals are more comprehensive than others (MahaRERA is the gold standard).

### Can I do due diligence for free?

Yes. RERA portals, court records (eCourts), and property registration data (IGR) are free. The only cost is a title search through a lawyer (Rs 15,000-50,000) which is worth the investment.
