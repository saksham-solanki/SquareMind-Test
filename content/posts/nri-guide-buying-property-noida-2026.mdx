## Why NRIs Are Investing in Noida in 2026

Noida offers the most affordable entry point among Delhi NCR cities with strong appreciation potential. The Noida International Airport (Jewar) construction, Aqua Line Metro, and Film City development are transforming the investment landscape. NRIs looking for high-growth bets in NCR increasingly choose Noida and Greater Noida.

### Emerging Investment Zones

Jewar Airport zone, Knowledge Park V, and sectors along the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway offer entry below Rs 4,000/sq ft with strong 3-5 year appreciation potential as airport becomes operational.

## Uttar Pradesh Stamp Duty and Registration

UP charges 5-7% stamp duty (5% for women, 7% for men). Registration charge is 1% of property value. Women buyers get a meaningful 2% concession, making joint registration with female family members advantageous.

## Top Builders in Noida

Supertech (select projects), ATS Infrastructure, Gaur Group, and Godrej Properties. Noida has had delivery issues with several builders — due diligence is critical. Verify:

1. UP-RERA registration on up-rera.in
2. Builder's actual delivery track record (not just brochure claims)
3. Existing society maintenance and handover status
4. NRI home loan bank tie-ups

## FEMA Compliance

Standard [FEMA rules](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Noida-specific:

- Noida Authority allotment status must be verified (leasehold vs freehold)
- Many Noida properties are leasehold from Noida Authority — understand renewal terms
- Agricultural land along Yamuna Expressway requires conversion verification
- POA must be Consulate-attested

## NRI Tax Implications

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax liability.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS. Noida yields of 3-4% from IT/corporate tenants
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG. 20% TDS by buyer on sale
- **Leasehold implications:** Transfer fee to Noida Authority on resale (2.5% typically)

## Property Verification

1. **Title search** and **allotment letter** from Noida Authority
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from UP Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **UP-RERA registration** verification
4. **Occupation Certificate** from Noida Authority
5. **Lease renewal status** and transfer fee obligations
