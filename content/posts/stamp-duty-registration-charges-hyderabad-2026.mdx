## Hyderabad Stamp Duty Rates (2026)

Telangana offers competitive stamp duty rates after the 2021 reduction:

| Component | Rate |
| --- | --- |
| Stamp duty | 4% |
| Transfer duty | 1.5% |
| Registration charge | 0.5% |
| **Total** | **6%** |

No gender-based concession in Telangana. The same rates apply to all buyers regardless of gender.

## Market Value System

Telangana uses a "Market Value" system (equivalent to circle rate) set by the Registration and Stamps Department. Updated annually. Stamp duty calculated on the higher of: transaction value or market value.

Check market values: registration.telangana.gov.in

## Online Registration Process

Telangana has one of the most digitized registration systems in India:

1. Visit registration.telangana.gov.in
2. Slot booking for registration
3. Enter property details and calculate stamp duty
4. Pay stamp duty online through e-Stamping
5. Visit Sub-Registrar Office (SRO) for biometric registration
6. Registered document available for download within 24-48 hours
