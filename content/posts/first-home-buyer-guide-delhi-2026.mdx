## Your First Home in Delhi NCR: Where to Start

Delhi NCR offers a vast range of options from DDA flats in Delhi to new projects in Noida Extension and Dwarka Expressway. First-time buyers need to balance budget, commute, and growth potential.

## Budget-Friendly Areas

| Area | Price Range (2BHK) | Metro Access | Key Advantage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dwarka (Sector 21-25) | Rs 55L-90L | Yes (Blue Line) | Planned, metro-connected |
| Noida Extension (Gaur City) | Rs 30L-55L | Coming (Aqua extension) | Most affordable NCR |
| Raj Nagar Extension (Ghaziabad) | Rs 25L-45L | Yes (Red Line nearby) | Budget-friendly |
| Greater Noida West | Rs 28L-50L | Coming | Affordable, growing |
| Faridabad (Sector 75-89) | Rs 35L-60L | Yes (Violet Line) | Delhi metro connected |
| Dwarka Expressway (Sector 99-113) | Rs 45L-80L | Coming | New corridor, appreciation |

## DDA Flats: An Alternative

DDA periodically releases affordable housing schemes. Advantages: government-backed, no builder risk, prime Delhi locations. Disadvantages: quality varies, waiting period, limited customization.

Check dda.gov.in for current schemes.

## Stamp Duty Advantage

Delhi offers generous women buyer concessions (2% lower). On a Rs 60L flat with a female buyer: stamp duty at 4% (Rs 2.4L) vs 6% (Rs 3.6L) for male. Savings: Rs 1.2L.

Use our [Stamp Duty Calculator](/tools/stamp-duty-calculator) to calculate your exact costs.

## Step-by-Step Process

1. Calculate affordability: [Buy vs Rent Calculator](/tools/buy-vs-rent) and [EMI Calculator](/tools/emi-calculator)
2. Get home loan pre-approval from 2 banks
3. Shortlist 3-4 areas matching budget and commute
4. Visit at least 5 projects, compare
5. Verify RERA registration and title
6. Negotiate and book
7. Complete registration at Sub-Registrar
