## Pune: The IT Professional's Preferred First Home City

Pune combines strong IT job market, pleasant climate, excellent education infrastructure, and prices 30-40% lower than Mumbai. For first-time buyers, it offers the ideal balance of career growth and homeownership affordability.

## Cooperative Housing: Pune's Unique System

Many Pune apartments are in cooperative housing societies. First-time buyers should know:

- Society share certificate is issued (in addition to sale deed)
- Monthly maintenance decided by society members
- NOC from society required for resale
- Transfer premium charged by society on resale (Rs 25,000-1L)

## Step-by-Step Process

1. Affordability check: [Buy vs Rent Calculator](/tools/buy-vs-rent)
2. Home loan pre-approval from 2 banks
3. Shortlist areas (commute + budget balance)
4. Visit projects, verify MahaRERA registration
5. Check society status (for resale) or builder track record (new)
6. Negotiate (5-10% negotiation in Pune is standard)
7. Register at Sub-Registrar
