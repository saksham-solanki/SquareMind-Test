## Project Highlights

### My Home Tridasa — Best Value in Western Corridor

My Home Group has established itself as Hyderabad's most reliable premium developer. Tridasa in Tellapur offers ORR connectivity, 2BHK starting at ₹55L, and My Home's signature construction quality. The developer's track record includes on-time delivery for 90%+ of past projects.

**Best for:** Investors seeking western corridor exposure at 20–30% below Gachibowli pricing.

### Aparna Sarovar Zenith — Mid-Segment Champion

Aparna Constructions dominates Hyderabad's mid-segment with consistent quality and pricing. Sarovar Zenith in Nallagandla offers proximity to the Financial District at ₹6,800–8,500/sqft with gated community amenities.

**Best for:** End-users and investors wanting a balance of quality, location, and affordability.

### Sumadhura Eden Garden — Yield-Focused Pick

In Kompally, Sumadhura's Eden Garden offers 2BHK units starting at ₹35L — the lowest entry point on our list from a reputed builder. Rental yields of 3.4–3.8% make this a pure income play.

## Due Diligence Checklist

1. **RERA verification:** Check TS-RERA portal for registration status and reported delays
2. **HYDRAA clearance:** Confirm the project is not in any FTL or buffer zone
3. **Land title:** Ensure clear title with no litigation — particularly important for projects on converted agricultural land
4. **Approach road:** Many Hyderabad projects are marketed as "ORR-adjacent" but have poor last-mile connectivity
5. **Water source:** Verify if the project has HMWSSB (municipal) water connection or depends on borewells
