## Hyderabad: Digital Land Records but Old Issues

Telangana has modernised land records through the Dharani portal. However, legacy issues from Andhra Pradesh division and older land records still create complications.

## Step 1: TSRERA Verification

Telangana RERA registration is mandatory.

**How to check:** Visit [rera.telangana.gov.in](https://rera.telangana.gov.in). Search by project name or registration number. Verify carpet area, possession date, and promoter details.

Use our [RERA Verification Tool](/tools/rera-verifier) for guided verification.

## Step 2: Dharani Portal Land Records

The Dharani portal (dharani.telangana.gov.in) is Telangana's integrated land records system.

**What to check:**
- Passbook (replaces old Pattadar Passbook)
- Land ownership details and survey number
- Transaction history
- Encumbrance status

**Key advantage:** Dharani integrates registration and mutation — property transfer is reflected immediately.

## Step 3: GHMC / HMDA Approval

- GHMC (Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation) approval for projects within city limits
- HMDA (Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority) approval for outer areas
- Building permission and layout approval
- Occupancy certificate after construction

## Step 4: Encumbrance Certificate

- Available from Sub-Registrar office
- Online via Dharani portal for recent transactions
- Check for 30 years minimum

## Red Flags to Watch For

1. **Government land encroachment** — common in outer Hyderabad, especially near ORR
2. **FTL (Full Tank Level) and buffer zone violations** — properties near lakes may be in restricted zones
3. **Revenue vs Municipal land classification** — ensure consistency
4. **Old AP-era disputes** — bifurcation issues between Telangana and AP
5. **Waqf Board land claims** — increasing in certain Hyderabad localities

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How reliable is the Dharani portal?

Dharani is one of India's best digital land record systems. However, it only covers post-migration records reliably. For older transactions, manual verification at the sub-registrar office is still recommended.

### What is FTL restriction in Hyderabad?

Full Tank Level (FTL) is the maximum water level of a lake. No construction is permitted within the FTL boundary. GHMC increasingly enforces this — verify your property is outside FTL zones.
