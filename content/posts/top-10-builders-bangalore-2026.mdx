## Methodology

Rankings based on: RERA delivery track record (40%), financial health (25%), construction quality (20%), buyer feedback (15%). Independent analysis — no builder advertising.

## Bangalore Builder Rankings 2026

### 1. Sobha Limited — Grade A

| Metric | Score |
| --- | --- |
| On-time delivery | 67.9% |
| Debt-to-Equity | 1.6x |
| Quality rating | 9.5/10 |
| Buyer satisfaction | 9.0/10 |

Industry-best delivery and construction quality. Backward-integrated model ensures consistency. 10-20% price premium is justified for self-use.

### 2. Prestige Group — Grade A-

| Metric | Score |
| --- | --- |
| On-time delivery | 50.0% |
| Debt-to-Equity | 1.5x |
| Quality rating | 8.8/10 |
| Buyer satisfaction | 8.5/10 |

Bangalore's largest developer. Strong across Whitefield, Sarjapur, and Devanahalli. Township developments are excellent.

### 3. Brigade Group — Grade B+

| Metric | Score |
| --- | --- |
| On-time delivery | 47.1% |
| Debt-to-Equity | 1.2x |
| Quality rating | 8.2/10 |
| Buyer satisfaction | 8.0/10 |

Reliable mid-tier developer with diversified portfolio including hotels. North Bangalore and Whitefield are strongest.

### 4. Puravankara — Grade B

| Metric | Score |
| --- | --- |
| On-time delivery | 40.0% |
| Debt-to-Equity | 1.3x |
| Quality rating | 7.8/10 |
| Buyer satisfaction | 7.5/10 |

Solid developer with good affordable housing brand (Provident). Competitive pricing.

### 5. Embassy Group — Grade B

Strong commercial developer with growing residential presence. Projects near Embassy tech parks are best positioned.

### 6. Godrej Properties — Grade B+

Pan-India developer with growing Bangalore presence. Good for investors wanting listed company transparency.

### 7. Birla Estates — Grade B

New entrant with strong Aditya Birla backing. Birla Alokya is well-received. Limited track record.

### 8. Salarpuria Sattva — Grade B

Long-standing Bangalore developer. Strong in East Bangalore. Mix of residential and commercial.

### 9. Total Environment — Grade B+

Boutique luxury developer. Exceptional design quality. Very small portfolio. Delivery times can be long.

### 10. Assetz Property — Grade B-

Growing developer with good micro-market selection. Affordable luxury positioning.

## Key Takeaway

Bangalore is fortunate to have several above-average developers. Sobha and Prestige set the standard. For affordable segment, Provident by Puravankara is the standout.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is the best builder in Bangalore?

Sobha Limited has the best delivery track record (67.9%) and construction quality. Prestige Group has the largest portfolio and strongest brand. Both are excellent choices.

### Which Bangalore locality has the best builders?

Whitefield and Sarjapur Road have the highest concentration of Grade-A developer projects. Devanahalli is emerging with Prestige and Godrej launches.
