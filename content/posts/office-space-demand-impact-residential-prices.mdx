## The Office-Residential Price Connection

In Indian metros, residential property prices are strongly correlated with nearby commercial development. IT parks, SEZs, and business districts create employment density that directly drives housing demand and prices.

## The Pattern

For every 1 million sqft of new office space, surrounding residential properties see:

| Distance from Office Hub | Price Impact | Timeline |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0-3 km | +12-18% | 2-3 years |
| 3-7 km | +8-12% | 3-5 years |
| 7-15 km | +4-8% | 5-7 years |

## Return-to-Office Impact (2024-2026)

Post-pandemic return-to-office mandates have amplified this effect:

| Metric | 2022 (Hybrid) | 2025 (Mostly Office) | Change |

| Rental demand near offices | Moderate | Very high | +45% |
| Residential prices near IT parks | Recovering | Peak | +30-40% |
