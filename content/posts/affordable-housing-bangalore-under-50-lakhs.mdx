## What to Avoid

1. **Remote locations without employment anchors:** Nelamangala, Doddaballapur, and outer Anekal lack nearby IT clusters — rental demand is weak
2. **Unapproved layouts:** Some affordable Bangalore projects sit on unapproved BDA/BBMP layouts. Verify before buying
3. **Borewell-dependent projects:** Bangalore's water crisis means projects without Cauvery connection will face increasing tanker costs

## The Bottom Line

Bangalore under ₹50 lakhs works best in Electronic City Phase 2, Yelahanka, and Attibele — locations with IT employment within 20 minutes and mid-tier builder options. The yields (3.0-4.0%) rival premium corridor investments at a fraction of the capital outlay.
