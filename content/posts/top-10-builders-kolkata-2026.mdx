## Kolkata: Affordable Metro, Growing Market

Kolkata remains India's most affordable metro for property. Entry prices start at ₹3,000/sqft in peripheral areas — significantly lower than any other major city.

## Kolkata Market Note

Kolkata has lower property prices but also lower appreciation rates. The market is driven by end-use demand more than investment speculation. Rental yields are moderate (3-4%).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Kolkata good for real estate investment?

Kolkata offers the lowest entry prices among metros. Appreciation is moderate but consistent. Best suited for affordable housing investments or end-use purchases.

### Which area in Kolkata is best for buying property?

New Town/Rajarhat for IT-driven growth, EM Bypass for connectivity, and South Kolkata for established lifestyle. Prices range from ₹3,500 to ₹12,000/sqft depending on locality.
