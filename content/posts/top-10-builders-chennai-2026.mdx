## Chennai: Conservative Market, Reliable Builders

Chennai's real estate market is characterised by conservative pricing, strong end-user demand, and relatively better builder discipline compared to Mumbai or NCR.

## Chennai-Specific Guidance

Tamil Nadu requires Patta and Chitta documents for land verification. EC (Encumbrance Certificate) check is crucial — Chennai has complex land records. Always verify with sub-registrar office.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is Chennai's best builder?

Casagrand is the largest local developer. Prestige and Sobha offer better quality but fewer options. TVS Emerald is excellent but limited portfolio.

### Is OMR still a good investment corridor?

OMR remains Chennai's primary IT corridor with strong rental demand. Newer OMR (Kelambakkam-Siruseri) offers better entry pricing. Established OMR (Perungudi-Sholinganallur) offers lower risk.
