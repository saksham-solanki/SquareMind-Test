## NCR's Three Investment Corridors

Delhi NCR offers three distinct real estate micro-markets, each with different price points, growth catalysts, and risk profiles. Understanding these differences is essential for NCR property investment.

## Price Spectrum

| Parameter | Gurgaon | Noida | Greater Noida |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Average price/sqft | Rs 8,000-22,000 | Rs 4,500-10,000 | Rs 2,800-6,000 |
| Premium segment | Rs 18,000-45,000 | Rs 8,000-15,000 | Rs 5,000-8,000 |
| 2BHK cost | Rs 80L-2.5 Cr | Rs 35L-90L | Rs 20L-50L |
| YoY appreciation | 8-12% | 10-15% | 12-18% |
| Rental yield | 2.5-3.5% | 3.0-4.5% | 3.5-5.0% |

Greater Noida offers the lowest entry point with highest appreciation potential — but carries the most risk.

## Infrastructure Catalysts

### Gurgaon
- Dwarka Expressway (SPR) — near completion, major price catalyst for sectors 85-115
- Golf Course Extension Road — premium corridor development
- KMP Expressway connectivity — industrial and logistics growth
- Established corporate campuses — Cyber Hub, DLF Cyber City, Udyog Vihar

### Noida
- Jewar International Airport — scheduled 2025-26 opening, 30km from Noida
- Noida Metro extension to Greater Noida
- Film City development in Sector 21
- Aqua Line Metro operational
- Sector 150-168 emerging as premium residential zone

### Greater Noida
- Direct Jewar Airport proximity — 15-20km
- Yamuna Expressway industrial corridor
- Greater Noida West (Noida Extension) affordable housing cluster
- Night Safari and proposed sports city
- Knowledge Park employment hub

## Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Gurgaon | Noida | Greater Noida |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Builder quality | High (DLF, Godrej, Tata) | Mixed | Risky (historical defaults) |
| Water/power | Moderate issues | Better than Gurgaon | Good (newer infrastructure) |
| Resale liquidity | Excellent | Good | Low-moderate |
| Regulatory | Haryana RERA | UP-RERA | UP-RERA |
| Completion risk | Low | Moderate | High |

## Which NCR Market Fits Your Profile?

**Gurgaon:** Premium buyer seeking corporate rental income, established infrastructure, and capital preservation. Budget Rs 1 Cr+.

**Noida:** Value seeker wanting Jewar Airport upside with reasonable builder quality. Budget Rs 35L-1 Cr.

**Greater Noida:** Aggressive investor willing to accept higher risk for highest percentage returns. Budget Rs 20L-50L. Only buy from RERA-verified builders with delivery track records.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Greater Noida safe to invest in after the Amrapali, Jaypee issues?

Post-RERA, Greater Noida has improved significantly. However, stick to builders with completed project delivery records. Avoid pre-launch offers from unproven developers. Check UP-RERA for complaint history before investing.

### Will Dwarka Expressway push Gurgaon prices higher?

Yes. Sectors along SPR (Southern Peripheral Road) have already seen 30-40% appreciation since construction began. Post-completion, expect another 10-15% appreciation as connectivity improves commute times.

### How close should I buy to Jewar Airport?

Properties within 15-30km of Jewar Airport along the Yamuna Expressway offer the best risk-reward balance. Avoid properties too close (noise zone) or too far (75km+, where connectivity benefits diminish).
