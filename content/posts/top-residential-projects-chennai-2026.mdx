## Chennai's Developer Landscape: Local Champions Lead

Chennai's residential market is dominated by local developers who understand the city's unique buyer preferences — vastu compliance, ventilation focus, and value-for-money positioning. Casagrand, Radiance, and Navin's lead in volume, while Prestige, TVS Emerald, and Godrej bring national-grade quality.

## Project Highlights

### Casagrand Zenith — Best Value on OMR

Casagrand is Chennai's largest developer by volume and has consistently delivered quality projects on time. Zenith in Siruseri offers OMR IT corridor proximity at ₹5,000-6,500/sqft with a comprehensive amenity package.

**Best for:** IT professional investors seeking rental income with SIPCOT IT Park as the demand anchor.

### Prestige Courtyards — Premium OMR Play

Prestige's Chennai presence brings Bangalore-grade construction quality to Sholinganallur. At ₹7,200-9,000/sqft, it's premium for Chennai but offers Prestige's brand resale premium.

### TVS Emerald Aaranya — Trusted Corporate Backing

TVS Emerald is backed by the TVS Group, providing financial stability that few Chennai developers can match. Aaranya in Perumbakkam offers nature-themed living at mid-range pricing.
