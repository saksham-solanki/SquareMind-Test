## Your First Home in Bangalore: The IT Professional's Guide

Most first-time buyers in Bangalore are IT professionals in their late 20s to mid-30s. The key decision: proximity to your tech park vs affordability. Here is how to find the right balance.

## IT Corridor Strategy

**Whitefield belt:** Hoskote, Budigere Cross, Kadugodi — Rs 30-50L for 2BHK. Metro Purple Line extension improves connectivity.

**ORR belt:** Sarjapur outer, Dommasandra, Chandapura — Rs 35-60L. Multiple tech parks within 15 km.

**North Bangalore:** Yelahanka, Thanisandra, Hennur — Rs 40-65L. Airport connectivity, Manyata proximity.

## Bangalore-Specific Checks

1. **A Khata vs B Khata:** Always buy A Khata property. B Khata means the property is not in the revenue records correctly — bank loans may be denied
2. **Land conversion:** Verify agricultural to residential conversion is complete
3. **RERA:** Check Karnataka RERA portal (rera.karnataka.gov.in)
4. **Betterment charges:** BDA layouts have betterment charges — factor these in
