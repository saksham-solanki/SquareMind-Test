## Why Women Should Be on the Property Title

India actively incentivizes women's property ownership through stamp duty concessions, lower loan rates, and PMAY benefits. Whether buying solo or as co-owner, having a woman on the title saves Rs 1-5 lakhs on most transactions.

**Strategy:** In states with concessions, register property in woman's name even if purchased with joint funds.

## PMAY Benefits for Women

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana mandates women ownership:
- **Credit-linked subsidy** of Rs 2.30-2.67 lakhs requires woman as owner or co-owner
- For EWS/LIG category, property must be in woman's name
- Joint ownership with husband is sufficient for MIG category

## Legal Protections

- **Hindu Succession Act (2005 amendment)** — daughters have equal coparcenary rights in ancestral property
- **Domestic Violence Act** — right to reside in shared household regardless of ownership
- **Maintenance laws** — courts can transfer property ownership as part of maintenance orders

## Practical Tips for Women Buyers

1. **Get the property in your name** — stamp duty savings and loan rate benefits compound
2. **Build independent credit history** — 6+ months of credit card/loan repayment history improves loan eligibility
3. **Check joint ownership implications** — joint ownership with spouse protects both parties in case of separation
4. **Consider term insurance** — home loan protection plan ensures property isn't lost if something happens to the earning partner

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can a non-working woman get a home loan?

Technically yes, but banks require income proof. A non-working woman can be a co-borrower (with earning spouse as primary borrower) and still be the property owner. This gets both the woman ownership benefits and loan approval.

### Is it better to be owner or co-owner?

Sole ownership gives complete control. Co-ownership (typically with spouse) provides stamp duty benefits in some states while allowing both parties' income for loan eligibility. For first-time purchases, co-ownership is usually optimal.

### Do stamp duty concessions apply to resale properties?

Yes. Stamp duty concessions for women apply to both new and resale property purchases. The concession is on the buyer's gender, not the property type.
