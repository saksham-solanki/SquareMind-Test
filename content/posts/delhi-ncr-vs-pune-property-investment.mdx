## Two Markets, Very Different Risk Profiles

Delhi NCR and Pune compete for the same buyer segment — IT professionals and corporate employees seeking value outside Mumbai. But the markets have fundamentally different risk profiles.

## Builder Quality Comparison

| Metric | Delhi NCR | Pune |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Project abandonment rate | High (esp. Noida) | Very low |
| RERA enforcement | Moderate (two states) | Strong (MahaRERA) |
| Listed developer presence | Good (Gurgaon) | Good |
| Average delivery delay | 18-24 months | 8-14 months |
| Buyer complaint rate | High | Moderate |

**Clear winner: Pune.** MahaRERA is India's most active regulator. NCR splits across Haryana RERA and UP RERA with less consistent enforcement. Noida has had the worst builder default record in the country.

## Our Verdict

**Pune wins on risk-adjusted returns.** Better RERA enforcement, lower builder default risk, comparable appreciation, and better rental yields make Pune the safer bet. NCR offers higher upside in select Gurgaon corridors but with significantly more risk.

**Exception:** Gurgaon Golf Course Road and established DLF sectors are world-class locations that Pune cannot match for prestige and liquidity. If budget allows ₹2 Cr+, Gurgaon premium is justified.
