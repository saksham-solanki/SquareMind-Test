## Bangalore's Builder Landscape: Premium Players Dominate

Bangalore's residential market is dominated by four national-grade developers: Prestige Group, Sobha Limited, Brigade Group, and Godrej Properties. Together they account for over 40% of premium launches. Their presence ensures construction quality, RERA compliance, and reasonable delivery timelines — making Bangalore one of India's safest markets for project-level investing.

## Project Highlights

### Prestige City — Bangalore's Landmark Township

Prestige City on Sarjapur-Marathahalli Road is a 180-acre integrated township with commercial, retail, and residential components. At ₹7,500-9,800/sqft, it offers Prestige's premium quality at competitive pricing for the location. The scale ensures long-term value appreciation and amenity maturity.

**Best for:** Investors seeking a safe, liquid investment with Prestige brand premium ensuring 10-15% higher resale vs comparable projects.

### Brigade El Dorado — Best Value Pick

Brigade El Dorado in Bagalur (North Bangalore) offers Brigade's national-grade quality at ₹5,200-6,800/sqft. The location benefits from Manyata Tech Park proximity and upcoming metro expansion. At sub-₹50L for a 2BHK, it's the most accessible entry point from a top-4 Bangalore builder.

**Best for:** Budget investors wanting top-builder quality with North Bangalore appreciation potential.

### Sobha Dream Acres — ORR Quality Without ORR Pricing

Sobha's construction quality is widely regarded as Bangalore's best. Dream Acres near Panathur offers Sobha quality at ₹8,200-10,500/sqft — 20% below comparable ORR projects. Strong rental demand from nearby tech parks.
