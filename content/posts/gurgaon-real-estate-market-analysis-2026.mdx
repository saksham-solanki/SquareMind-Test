## Gurgaon's Market in 2026: Post-Infrastructure Maturation

Gurgaon's real estate market is entering a new phase. The infrastructure build-out that drove 15-20% annual appreciation in 2022-2025 (Dwarka Expressway, metro extension, road widening) is now largely complete or priced in. The market is transitioning from infrastructure-driven growth to employment-driven growth.

This doesn't mean the opportunity is gone — it means the playbook has changed. In 2026, the winning strategy is following corporate expansion, not infrastructure announcements.

## Supply Dynamics

### Developer Activity
Gurgaon saw 48,000 new residential launches in 2025. DLF remains the dominant force with 30% market share in premium segment. Godrej, Sobha, M3M, and Tata Housing are the other major players.

### Absorption Rates
Overall absorption: 62-68%. Dwarka Expressway absorption has slowed to 58% (from 72% in 2023) as prices have risen significantly. New Gurgaon maintains healthy 68-72% absorption.

### Inventory Overhang
Gurgaon has approximately 18 months of unsold inventory — healthy by NCR standards but up from 14 months in 2024. Some Dwarka Expressway sub-sectors show early signs of oversupply.

## Predictions for 2026-2028

**Base case (50%):** Corporate expansion continues, infrastructure maintenance improves, no new major catalyst. Dwarka Expressway 8-12%, New Gurgaon 10-14%, Golf Course 5-8%.

**Bull case (25%):** Delhi-Gurgaon metro expansion announced, major new corporate campus, interest rate cuts. Across-the-board 14-18%.

**Bear case (25%):** Oversupply in Dwarka Expressway materialises, rate hikes, corporate hiring freeze. 3-6% appreciation, some pockets flat.

## The Bottom Line

Gurgaon remains NCR's premium real estate market but is transitioning from infrastructure-driven to employment-driven growth. New Gurgaon (Sec 76-95) offers the best 2026 entry point for balanced returns. Dwarka Expressway is mature but still has legs. Premium Golf Course corridor is for end-users and wealth preservation, not active investment.
