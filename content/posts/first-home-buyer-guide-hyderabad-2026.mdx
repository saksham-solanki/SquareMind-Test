## Hyderabad: Best Value for First-Time Buyers

Hyderabad offers the best combination of affordability, job market strength, and low transaction costs among India's top metros. With stamp duty at just 4% and property prices 30-50% lower than Bangalore, it is the most first-time buyer-friendly city.

## Affordable Areas

| Area | Price Range (2BHK) | Nearest IT Hub | Key Advantage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kompally | Rs 30L-50L | Hitech City (40 min) | Affordable, growing |
| Bachupally | Rs 35L-55L | Gachibowli (30 min) | IT corridor access |
| Pocharam/Ghatkesar | Rs 25L-45L | Uppal IT corridor | ORR access, affordable |
| Tellapur | Rs 35L-55L | Gachibowli (20 min) | Close to Financial District |
| Pragathi Nagar | Rs 35L-60L | Hitech City (25 min) | Established residential |
| Narsingi | Rs 40L-65L | Financial District (10 min) | Premium affordable |

## Low Transaction Cost Advantage

Hyderabad's 6% total transaction cost (4% stamp duty + 1.5% transfer + 0.5% registration) is among the lowest in India. Compare:

| City | Total Transaction Cost on Rs 60L Property |
| --- | --- |
| Hyderabad | Rs 3.6L |
| Bangalore | Rs 4.9L |
| Mumbai | Rs 4.8L |
| Chennai | Rs 6.6L |
