## Three Cities, Three Investment Profiles

Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad are India's three most active real estate investment markets. Each serves a different investor profile. This comparison uses data to help you decide.

## Price Comparison

| Metric | Mumbai | Bangalore | Hyderabad |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Avg price (₹/sqft) | ₹18,500 | ₹8,800 | ₹7,500 |
| Entry price (₹/sqft) | ₹7,000 | ₹5,000 | ₹4,000 |
| Avg 2BHK cost | ₹1.2 Cr | ₹75 L | ₹55 L |
| 5-Year CAGR | 8.2% | 11.5% | 13.2% |
| Gross rental yield | 3.2% | 4.4% | 4.7% |

**Key finding:** Hyderabad has delivered the highest absolute appreciation over 5 years. Bangalore is a close second. Mumbai offers lower percentage returns but higher absolute returns due to higher base prices.

## Which City for Which Investor?

**Mumbai** — Best for: Buyers with ₹1 Cr+ budget who want the safest, most liquid market. Lowest risk, lowest yield.

**Bangalore** — Best for: IT professionals who understand local micro-markets. Good balance of appreciation, yield, and safety.

**Hyderabad** — Best for: Value investors seeking highest appreciation at lowest entry cost. Higher risk from less established builders.
