## Ahmedabad: Low Entry Cost, High Growth Potential

Ahmedabad offers first-time buyers a rare combination: affordable property prices, low stamp duty (4.9%), and strong economic growth driven by GIFT City and Gujarat's industrial ecosystem.

## Low Transaction Cost Advantage

Ahmedabad's 5.9% total transaction cost is among India's lowest:
- Stamp duty: 4.9%
- Registration: 1%
- No metro cess or surcharge

On a Rs 40L flat: Total transaction cost is Rs 2.36L. Compare with Mumbai (Rs 3.6L) or Chennai (Rs 4.4L).

## Ahmedabad-Specific Tips

1. **TP Scheme:** Verify Town Planning scheme for the area — impacts future development
2. **NA permission:** For plots in outskirts, verify Non-Agricultural conversion
3. **Gujarat RERA:** Check gujrera.gujarat.gov.in for project registration
4. **Jantri value:** Understand that stamp duty is on jantri or actual value (whichever higher)
