## FDI in Indian Real Estate: The Big Picture

Foreign direct investment in Indian real estate has been growing steadily since 2005 liberalisation. Total FDI in the sector crossed $58 billion cumulatively by 2025.

## Impact on Property Prices

FDI impacts property prices through several channels:

1. **Commercial absorption** — FDI-funded office spaces create employment hubs that drive residential demand
2. **Quality benchmarking** — foreign investors demand higher construction and governance standards
3. **Liquidity injection** — institutional capital allows developers to fund projects without excessive debt
4. **Price floor** — institutional investment creates price floors in commercial segments

## What It Means for Buyers

FDI-heavy areas typically see: better infrastructure, higher construction standards, but also premium pricing. **Follow the FDI** is a legitimate investment strategy — areas attracting institutional capital tend to appreciate faster.
