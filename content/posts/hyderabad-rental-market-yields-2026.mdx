## Hyderabad Rental Market

Hyderabad's rental market is driven by IT sector demand concentrated in the western corridor. Return-to-office mandates have pushed rents up significantly from pandemic lows.

## Key Insight

Hyderabad offers 4.5-5.1% gross rental yields — among India's best. The combination of strong IT rental demand and lower property prices creates superior yield compared to Mumbai (3.0-3.5%) and even Bangalore (4.0-4.7%). Manikonda offers the best yield at 5.1%.
