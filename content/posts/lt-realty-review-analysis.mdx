## Engineering DNA in Real Estate

L&T Realty is the real estate development arm of Larsen & Toubro — India's largest engineering and construction company. When India's best builder of infrastructure does residential, the results are interesting.

## Company Overview

- **Subsidiary of L&T** (NSE: LT)
- **Headquarters:** Mumbai
- **Active cities:** Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad
- **Key projects:** Crescent Bay (Mumbai), Raintree Boulevard (Bangalore), Eden Park (Chennai)
- **Revenue:** Part of L&T consolidated — not separately disclosed

## RERA Delivery Track Record

Analysis of 12 L&T Realty projects across MahaRERA and Karnataka RERA (2017-2023).

| Category | Projects | % of Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Delivered on time | 6 | 50.0% |
| Delayed 6–18 months | 4 | 33.3% |
| Delayed 18–36 months | 2 | 16.7% |
| Significantly delayed | 0 | 0% |

**Assessment:** 50% on-time delivery with zero abandoned or significantly delayed projects. L&T's engineering capability shows in execution — they build what they promise, sometimes just later than promised.

## Construction Quality

This is where L&T Realty genuinely differentiates:

- **Structural engineering** — superior to virtually all developers; L&T builds metro systems and nuclear plants
- **MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing)** — best-in-class
- **Common areas** — well-designed and durable
- **Premium specifications** — solid but not ostentatious

## Our Verdict

**Overall Grade: B+**

L&T Realty offers engineering-grade construction quality with strong corporate backing. Limited portfolio and premium pricing are the main constraints. Best for buyers who value structural quality and brand safety.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is L&T Realty a good builder?

L&T Realty brings India's strongest engineering capability to residential construction. Structural quality is best-in-class. Delivery timelines are good but not exceptional.

### How does L&T Realty compare to other builders?

Construction quality is arguably the best in India due to L&T's engineering DNA. However, the portfolio is small, pricing is premium, and they operate in limited cities.
