## Why NRIs Are Investing in Delhi in 2026

Delhi's political and economic significance, combined with strong infrastructure spending (Phase IV Metro, Dwarka Expressway, Jewar Airport connectivity), makes it a consistently attractive market for NRI buyers. Property in Delhi offers both prestige value and solid rental returns from the government and corporate tenant base.

## Best Areas in Delhi for NRI Property Investment

### Premium Localities

Top NRI investment zones in Delhi include South Delhi (Greater Kailash, Defence Colony), Dwarka, Vasant Kunj, and Saket. These areas offer established infrastructure, security, and proximity to embassies and business districts.

| Locality | Price Range (per sq ft) | Rental Yield | Key Advantage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| South Delhi (GK/Defence Colony) | Rs 25,000-45,000 | 2-2.5% | Premium address, embassy proximity |
| Dwarka | Rs 8,000-14,000 | 3-3.5% | Metro connectivity, planned city |
| Vasant Kunj | Rs 12,000-20,000 | 2.5-3% | Proximity to airport, greenery |
| Saket | Rs 15,000-25,000 | 2.5-3% | Commercial hub, metro access |

### Emerging Investment Zones

Gurugram (Dwarka Expressway, New Gurugram), Noida (Sector 150, Jewar Airport zone), and Greater Noida West offer higher appreciation potential at 40-60% lower prices than central Delhi.

## Delhi Stamp Duty and Registration for NRI Buyers

Delhi charges 4-6% stamp duty depending on property value (4% for properties up to Rs 25 lakh, 6% above Rs 25 lakh). Women buyers get a 2% concession as the sole or first-named purchaser. Registration charge is 1% of property value.

## Top Builders in Delhi for NRI Buyers

Reputable developers with NRI desks include DLF, Emaar India, Tata Housing, and Godrej Properties. Always verify:

1. RERA registration on Delhi RERA portal
2. Builder's delivery track record in Delhi NCR
3. NRI documentation support and POA assistance
4. Bank tie-ups for NRI home loans

## FEMA Compliance for Delhi Property

All standard [FEMA rules for NRI property investment](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Delhi-specific points:

- Payments through NRE/NRO or direct foreign remittance only
- Agricultural land in Delhi outskirts requires RBI approval
- POA must be Consulate-attested and registered at Delhi Sub-Registrar
- DDA properties have additional transfer restrictions — verify before purchase

## NRI Tax Implications for Delhi Property

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax liability on rental income and capital gains.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS by tenant. File ITR for deductions and potential refund
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG without indexation. 20% TDS on sale by buyer
- **Section 54 exemption:** Reinvest gains in another residential property within 2 years

## Property Verification for Remote NRI Buyers

1. **Title search** from independent Delhi-based property lawyer
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from Delhi Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **Delhi RERA registration** verification online
4. **Building plan approval** from DDA/MCD
5. **Conversion certificate** (if formerly agricultural land)
