## Chennai: Stable Market, Higher Entry Cost

Chennai offers stable appreciation and diverse job market (IT, auto, manufacturing). However, Tamil Nadu's 11% stamp duty + registration makes it the most expensive city for transaction costs. First-time buyers need to plan for this.

## Managing High Stamp Duty

Tamil Nadu charges 11% total (7% stamp duty + 4% registration). Women save 1% (6% stamp duty).

On a Rs 45L flat:
- Male buyer: Rs 4.95L in stamp duty + registration
- Female buyer: Rs 4.50L
- Savings: Rs 45,000

## Chennai-Specific Verification

1. **CMDA/DTCP approval:** Only buy in approved layouts
2. **Patta and Chitta:** Must be in seller's name
3. **Encumbrance Certificate:** On tnreginet.gov.in
4. **TN-RERA:** Verify on tnrera.in
5. **No unapproved layouts** — common risk in Chennai suburbs
