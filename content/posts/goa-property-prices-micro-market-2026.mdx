## Goa Property Market 2026

Goa's real estate is a unique market driven by vacation homes, retirement living, and short-term rental income. Post-pandemic remote work culture has added a new buyer segment — digital nomads buying primary residences.

## Critical Warning: Land Title

Goa has unique land documentation challenges due to Portuguese-era records. Before any Goa property purchase:

1. Verify Comunidade land status — community-owned land has restricted transfer
2. Check CRZ (Coastal Regulation Zone) clearance
3. Confirm Form I & XIV from Mamlatdar office
4. Ensure TCP (Town & Country Planning) approval for construction
