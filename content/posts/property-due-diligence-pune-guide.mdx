## Pune: India's Most Registered Market

Pune has the highest number of RERA-registered projects in India. MahaRERA is the most active regulator. This works in buyers' favour — but verification is still essential.

## Step 1: MahaRERA Verification

Same process as Mumbai. Check maharera.maharashtra.gov.in. Pune projects are heavily regulated — most have complete quarterly updates.

Use our [RERA Verification Tool](/tools/rera-verifier) for guided verification.

## Step 2: 7/12 Extract and Property Card

**7/12 Extract** (Saat-Baara Utara) is the key land ownership document in Maharashtra.

- Available online at mahabhulekh.maharashtra.gov.in
- Contains: owner name, survey number, land type, area, crop pattern
- Verify the seller's name matches the 7/12 extract owner
- Check for any loans/liens recorded

**Property Card** — for urban areas within municipal limits. Available from City Survey Office.

## Step 3: NA Order (Non-Agricultural)

Many Pune outskirts properties require agricultural-to-residential conversion.

- Verify NA order from the District Collector
- Ensure NA order covers the specific survey number
- Check if NA is final order (not interim)
- Confirm NA is for residential use (not industrial/commercial)

## Step 4: PMC/PCMC/PMRDA Approval

| Authority | Jurisdiction |
| --- | --- |
| PMC (Pune Municipal Corporation) | Core Pune |
| PCMC (Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corp) | Pimpri-Chinchwad area |
| PMRDA (Pune Metro Region Dev Authority) | Outer Pune region |

Verify building permission, commencement certificate, and occupation certificate from the relevant authority.

## Red Flags to Watch For

1. **NA order not obtained** — common in Hinjewadi outskirts and Wagholi
2. **Multiple owners on 7/12** — joint ownership disputes can block sale
3. **Gaothan land** — village land within city limits with complex ownership
4. **Builder promises before RERA registration** — never pay before RERA is obtained
5. **Layout not approved by PMRDA** — illegal layout common in peripheral areas

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I check 7/12 extract online in Pune?

Visit mahabhulekh.maharashtra.gov.in. Select district (Pune), taluka, and village. Enter survey number. The 7/12 extract shows ownership, area, and any recorded encumbrances.

### Is PMRDA approval different from PMC?

Yes. PMRDA covers the wider Pune metropolitan region outside PMC/PCMC limits. Different building regulations apply. Verify which authority governs your property location.
