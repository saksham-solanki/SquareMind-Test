## Mumbai Property Prices: The Complete Picture

Mumbai remains India's most expensive property market. But prices vary dramatically by locality — from ₹8,000/sqft in extended suburbs to ₹1,50,000/sqft in South Mumbai. This guide maps every major micro-market.

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Malabar Hill | ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,50,000 | +4.2% | Ultra-luxury |
| Worli | ₹65,000 - ₹95,000 | +8.5% | Luxury |
| Lower Parel | ₹55,000 - ₹78,000 | +7.1% | Premium |
| Prabhadevi | ₹52,000 - ₹72,000 | +6.8% | Premium |
| Byculla | ₹28,000 - ₹42,000 | +12.3% | Redevelopment |

**Trend:** South Mumbai continues to command extreme premium. Worli and Lower Parel lead appreciation driven by new luxury launches (Oberoi, Lodha, Piramal). Byculla is emerging as a redevelopment hotspot.
