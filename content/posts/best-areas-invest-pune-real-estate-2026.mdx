## Pune: India's Most Liveable City Is Also Its Best-Value IT Corridor

Pune combines three things that make real estate investing work: a massive IT employment base (12 lakh+ professionals), affordable entry prices (40–60% below Mumbai), and a quality of life that keeps attracting migration. The city's real estate market has delivered consistent 10–15% annual appreciation across IT corridors since 2020.

The upcoming Pune Metro (Lines 1 and 2 operational, Line 3 planned) and the Mumbai–Pune Hyperloop corridor are the next major catalysts.

## Hinjewadi Phase 3 and Kharadi: Top Picks

### Hinjewadi Phase 3 — Growth Without the Congestion

Hinjewadi Phase 1 is congested and expensive. Phase 3, extending toward Maan and Mulshi, offers:

- Fresh inventory from Godrej, Kolte-Patil, and VTP Realty at ₹5,800–7,500/sqft
- Upcoming Hinjewadi–Shivajinagar Metro (Line 3) will halve commute times
- Ring Road connectivity improving access to Baner and Aundh
- 15–20% price discount vs Phase 1 for equivalent project quality

### Kharadi — The Eastern IT Hub

Kharadi's EON IT Park, World Trade Centre, and Magarpatta adjacency create deep employment demand. Metro Line 1 connectivity is operational, and prices at ₹6,500–8,800/sqft still have 20%+ appreciation runway.

**Key builders:** Panchshil, Nyati, Gera, Marvel — all with strong Pune track records.
