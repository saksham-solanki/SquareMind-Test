## Hyderabad: The New Real Estate Hotspot

Hyderabad has emerged as India's fastest-growing real estate market. Lower base prices, IT sector growth, and infrastructure development make it attractive. Here are the top builders operating in the city.

## Hyderabad Market Note

Hyderabad has no RERA-mandated carpet area standard, which means quoted areas may include balconies and terraces. Always verify carpet area definitions with the developer and check RERA registration.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is the biggest builder in Hyderabad?

My Home Group is Hyderabad's largest developer by active project count. Prestige Group and Brigade are the largest listed developers operating in the city.

### Is Hyderabad real estate still a good investment in 2026?

Hyderabad offers better value than Mumbai or Bangalore with strong IT sector growth driving demand. Financial District and Gachibowli are mature; Kokapet and Tellapur are emerging investment corridors.
