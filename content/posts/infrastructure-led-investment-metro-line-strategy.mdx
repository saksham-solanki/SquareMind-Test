## The Metro Line Strategy: India's Most Reliable Real Estate Play

Across every Indian city where metro lines have been built, a consistent pattern emerges: properties within 1 km of metro stations appreciate 20-35% above market average within 3 years of station opening. This is the most predictable investment strategy in Indian real estate.

## The Timing Framework

### Phase 1: Announcement (Maximum Risk, Maximum Return)
Buy when the metro route is announced but construction hasn't started. Prices are at base level. Risk: route changes, delays, cancellations. Return potential: 40-60% above market.

### Phase 2: Construction (Moderate Risk, Good Return)
Buy when construction is visible. Route confirmed, but completion date uncertain. Risk: delays. Return potential: 25-35% above market.

### Phase 3: Pre-Completion (Low Risk, Moderate Return)
Buy 6-12 months before station opening. Route certain, timeline clear. Risk: minimal. Return potential: 15-25%.

### Phase 4: Post-Opening (Lowest Risk, Priced In)
Buy after station opens. The premium is largely reflected in prices. Return: market average going forward.

**Our recommendation:** Phase 2 (construction visible) offers the best risk-adjusted entry point.
