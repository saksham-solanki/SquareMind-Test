## Why NRIs Are Investing in Hyderabad in 2026

Hyderabad offers the best value-for-money proposition among India's top metros for NRI investors. With Telangana's proactive IT policies (attracting Apple, Amazon, Google campuses), lower stamp duty at 4%, and no circle rate inflation (market rates align closely with registration rates), NRIs get transparent pricing and strong appreciation.

## Best Areas in Hyderabad for NRI Property Investment

### Premium Localities

Top investment zones include Gachibowli, Hitech City, Kondapur, and Jubilee Hills.

| Locality | Price Range (per sq ft) | Rental Yield | Key Driver |

| Kondapur | Rs 6,000-10,000 | 3.5-4% | IT proximity, residential |

### Emerging Investment Zones

Kokapet, Narsingi, and Shamshabad (near airport) offer 25-40% lower prices with strong growth potential from the upcoming Regional Ring Road and Pharma City development.

## Telangana Stamp Duty and Registration

Telangana charges 4% stamp duty (reduced from 7.5% in 2021 — one of the lowest among major states). Registration charge is 0.5% additional. Transfer duty of 1.5% also applies, bringing total to around 6%.

## Top Builders in Hyderabad

My Home Group, Aparna Constructions, Rajapushpa Properties, and Phoenix Group. Verify:

1. TS-RERA registration on telangana.rera.gov.in
2. Builder delivery track record
3. NRI documentation support
4. Bank tie-ups for NRI home loans

## FEMA Compliance

Standard [FEMA rules](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Hyderabad-specific:

- Agricultural land (common in outer Hyderabad) requires RBI approval and land conversion
- Verify whether property falls under GHMC or HMDA jurisdiction
- POA must be Consulate-attested

## NRI Tax Implications

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax liability.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS. Hyderabad yields of 3-4% provide good rental returns
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG. 20% TDS on sale by buyer
- **Telangana property tax:** Must be paid annually via GHMC portal

## Property Verification

1. **Title search** from independent Hyderabad lawyer
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from Telangana Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **TS-RERA registration** verification
4. **NOC from Layout authority** (HMDA/GHMC)
5. **Land conversion certificate** for former agricultural plots
