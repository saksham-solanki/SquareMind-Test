## Why NRIs Are Investing in Pune in 2026

Pune combines IT-driven rental demand with significantly lower entry prices than Mumbai. The Pune Metro, Hinjewadi-Shivajinagar Metro, and Mumbai-Pune Expressway expansion make it a strong appreciation play. Pune's quality of life (education, climate, safety) appeals to NRIs planning eventual return.

### Emerging Investment Zones

Wagholi, Undri, and Keshav Nagar offer 25-40% lower prices with Pune Metro connectivity improving access. Hinjewadi Phase 3 and Mahalunge-Maan corridor are strong bets for 2026-2028 appreciation.

## Maharashtra Stamp Duty for Pune

Same Maharashtra Stamp Act applies — 6% stamp duty in Pune (5% for women as sole/first-named purchaser). Registration charge 1%, capped at Rs 30,000. Metro cess of 1% applies within PMC limits.

## Top Builders in Pune

Kolte Patil Developers, Kumar Properties, Gera Developments, and VTP Realty. Verify:

1. MahaRERA registration on maharera.mahaonline.gov.in
2. Builder delivery history in Pune
3. NRI support for documentation and POA
4. NRI home loan bank tie-ups

## FEMA Compliance

Standard [FEMA rules](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Pune-specific:

- Agricultural/NA (Non-Agricultural) conversion status must be verified for plots outside PMC limits
- Pune has many cooperative housing societies — NRI membership has additional documentation requirements
- POA must be Consulate-attested and registered at Maharashtra Sub-Registrar

## NRI Tax Implications

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax on rental income and capital gains.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS. Pune IT corridor yields 3-4.5%
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG. Apply for lower TDS certificate under Section 197
- **Property tax:** PMC/PCMC property tax payable annually

## Property Verification

1. **Title search** from independent Pune lawyer
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from Maharashtra Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **MahaRERA registration** verification
4. **NA conversion order** for plots (critical in Pune outskirts)
5. **Society NOC** for resale flats in cooperative housing
