## The Real Cost: 15-25% Above Sale Price

When budgeting for your first home, the sale price is only 75-85% of your total cost. Here is every additional cost you need to plan for.

## One-Time Costs at Purchase

| Cost | Typical Amount | Paid To |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Stamp duty | 4-11% of property value | State government |
| Registration | 0.5-4% | State government |
| GST (under-construction only) | 5% on construction value | Central government |
| Brokerage | 1-2% | Broker |
| Legal fees (title search) | Rs 10,000-25,000 | Lawyer |
| Home loan processing fee | 0.25-1% of loan | Bank |
| Home insurance (optional) | Rs 5,000-15,000/year | Insurance company |
| Franking charges | Rs 100-500 | Franking agency |

## Example: Rs 70L Property in Bangalore

| Component | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Property price | Rs 70,00,000 |
| Stamp duty (5%) | Rs 3,50,000 |
| Registration (1%) | Rs 70,000 |
| Surcharge (2% above Rs 45L) | Rs 1,40,000 |
| Brokerage (1%) | Rs 70,000 |
| Legal fees | Rs 15,000 |
| Loan processing (0.5%) | Rs 28,000 |
| **Total one-time extra** | **Rs 6,73,000 (9.6%)** |

## Post-Purchase Costs

| Cost | Typical Amount | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Interior and furnishing | Rs 3-10L | One-time |
| Modular kitchen | Rs 1.5-4L | One-time |
| AC units (2-3) | Rs 1-2L | One-time |
| Moving charges | Rs 10,000-30,000 | One-time |

## Recurring Costs (Monthly/Annual)

| Cost | Typical Range | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Society maintenance | Rs 2,000-8,000/month | Monthly |
| Property tax | Rs 5,000-50,000/year | Annual |
| Home insurance | Rs 5,000-15,000/year | Annual |
| Repairs/maintenance | Rs 20,000-50,000/year | Annual |
| Home loan EMI | Based on loan amount | Monthly |

## The 20% Rule

Budget at least 20% above the property price for all additional costs. For a Rs 60L property, keep Rs 12L additional (Rs 72L total budget).
