## What to Watch Out For

1. **MahaRERA verification:** Always check MahaRERA portal for project registration and compliance
2. **Water source:** Ensure municipal water connection — many outer Pune projects depend on tankers
3. **Road connectivity:** Some affordable locations have good straight-line distance but poor road connectivity during peak hours
4. **Builder track record:** At this price point, verify the developer has completed at least 3 previous projects successfully

## The Bottom Line

Pune under ₹50 lakhs is a genuine opportunity — not a consolation. Wagholi and Hinjewadi Phase 3 offer IT corridor proximity, reputed builders, and yields above 3.5%. For first-time investors with limited budgets, Pune should be your first consideration.
