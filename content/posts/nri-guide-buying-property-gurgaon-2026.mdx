## Why NRIs Are Investing in Gurgaon in 2026

Gurgaon (Gurugram) is the corporate capital of North India with Fortune 500 headquarters, premium lifestyle offerings, and the highest rental yields in Delhi NCR. The Dwarka Expressway opening and Delhi-Mumbai Expressway connectivity have unlocked new growth corridors.

### Emerging Investment Zones

New Gurugram (Sector 82-95), SPR (Southern Peripheral Road), and sectors along the Delhi-Mumbai Expressway corridor offer entry at Rs 4,000-7,000 per sq ft with strong appreciation potential.

## Haryana Stamp Duty and Registration

Haryana charges 5-7% stamp duty (5% for properties up to Rs 50 lakhs, 7% above). Women get 2% concession as sole/first-named purchaser. Registration charge is Rs 50,000 flat (for urban areas).

## Top Builders in Gurgaon

DLF, M3M India, Godrej Properties, and Ireo. HRERA compliance is mandatory. Verify:

1. H-RERA registration on haryanarera.gov.in
2. Builder delivery record (Gurgaon has mixed builder track records — due diligence critical)
3. NRI documentation and POA support
4. NRI home loan partnerships

## FEMA Compliance

Standard [FEMA rules](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Gurgaon-specific:

- Colony licenses (Haryana-specific) must be verified for plotted developments
- Agricultural land (common beyond SPR) requires conversion verification
- POA must be Consulate-attested and registered at Haryana Sub-Registrar

## NRI Tax Implications

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax liability.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS. Gurgaon corporate tenants provide reliable 3-4% yields
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG. Apply for lower TDS certificate
- **Property tax:** MCG property tax payable annually

## Property Verification

1. **Title search** from independent Gurgaon lawyer
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from Haryana Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **H-RERA registration** verification
4. **Colony license** and CLU (Change of Land Use) verification
5. **Occupation Certificate** from DTCP Haryana
