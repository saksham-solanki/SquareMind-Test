## Hyderabad: India's Best Value-for-Money IT Market

Hyderabad's investment thesis is straightforward: IT employment growth rivalling Bangalore at 40-60% lower property prices. In 2026, the city has added 350+ GCCs, with Google, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft, and Wells Fargo expanding operations. The smart money recognises this value gap and is positioning ahead of the convergence.

## Top Investment Strategies

### Strategy 1: Kokapet-Tellapur Corridor

The emerging corridor between Gachibowli and the Financial District offers the best risk-adjusted entry in Hyderabad. My Home Group, Aparna, and Prestige have quality projects at ₹5,500-11,000/sqft — 20-30% below equivalent Gachibowli locations.

**Expected return:** 3.0-3.6% yield + 14-18% CAGR over 3-5 years.

### Strategy 2: Pharma City Long Position

Hyderabad Pharma City near Mucherla is a government-backed project that will create 100,000+ direct jobs. Properties in the surrounding corridor at ₹3,500-5,500/sqft offer pre-development pricing with significant upside potential.

**Risk:** 5-7 year timeline, dependent on government execution.

### Strategy 3: Kompally Yield Play

Kompally-Medchal at ₹4,000-6,000/sqft delivers 3.2-4.0% gross yield from IIT Hyderabad, DRDO, and pharma sector tenants. The lowest entry point for quality yield investing in a top-5 Indian metro.

## HYDRAA Mitigation

Post-HYDRAA, always verify: no FTL zone encroachment, HMDA/DTCP approval, clear land title. This due diligence adds a step but actually makes Hyderabad safer for investors by removing illegal constructions from the market.
