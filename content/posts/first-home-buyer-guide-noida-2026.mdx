## Noida: NCR's Most Affordable Entry Point

Noida and Greater Noida West (Noida Extension) offer the most affordable options in Delhi NCR for first-time buyers. The upcoming Jewar Airport adds a growth catalyst that few other markets have.

## Leasehold Reality

Most Noida properties are leasehold from Noida Authority. First-time buyers must understand:

- You hold a lease (30/90 years), not freehold ownership
- Transfer fee (2.5% of allotment rate) payable to Authority on purchase
- Lease renewal required at expiry
- Bank loans are available on leasehold (most banks accept)

## Women Buyer Savings

UP offers 2% concession for women. On a Rs 40L flat:
- Male: Rs 3.2L (8%)
- Female: Rs 2.4L (6%)
- **Savings: Rs 80,000**

## Builder Due Diligence (Critical in Noida)

Noida has had significant builder delivery issues. Before buying:

1. Check UP-RERA (up-rera.in) for project status and complaints
2. Visit builder's completed projects — check quality and maintenance
3. Verify if OC has been received for completed phases
4. Check for pending Noida Authority dues against the project
5. Talk to existing residents in builder's other projects
