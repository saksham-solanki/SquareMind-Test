## Infrastructure Giant Enters Housing

Adani Group — India's largest infrastructure conglomerate — has been expanding aggressively into residential real estate. This review examines their housing track record separately from the group's broader controversies.

## Company Overview

- **Subsidiary of Adani Group** (Adani Enterprises ecosystem)
- **Headquarters:** Ahmedabad
- **Active cities:** Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Gurgaon
- **Key projects:** Adani Western Heights (Mumbai), Shantigram (Ahmedabad), Oyster Grand (Gurgaon)
- **Revenue:** Part of Adani Group consolidated — not separately disclosed

## RERA Delivery Track Record

Analysis of 10 Adani Realty residential projects across MahaRERA, Gujarat RERA, and Haryana RERA (2017-2023).

| Category | Projects | % of Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Delivered on time | 4 | 40.0% |
| Delayed 6–18 months | 3 | 30.0% |
| Delayed 18–36 months | 2 | 20.0% |
| Significantly delayed | 1 | 10.0% |

**Assessment:** 40% on-time delivery is average for the industry. Limited residential track record. The infrastructure execution capability hasn't fully translated to residential delivery consistency yet.

## The Infrastructure Advantage

Adani's unique position:

- **Land bank access** — Adani Group's infrastructure projects (ports, airports, SEZs) provide adjacency to massive land holdings
- **Infrastructure development** — ability to build roads, utilities, and connectivity that other developers cannot
- **Shantigram (Ahmedabad)** — 800-acre township benefits directly from Adani infrastructure

## Risk Factors

1. **Group-level risk** — Adani Group's controversies and debt levels create headline risk that can affect property sentiment
2. **Limited residential track record** — infrastructure expertise doesn't automatically translate to residential
3. **Financial opacity** — residential arm financials not separately disclosed
4. **Brand perception** — polarising brand may affect resale in some markets

## Our Verdict

**Overall Grade: B-**

Adani Realty has strong infrastructure advantages and deep resources, but limited residential track record and group-level risk factors warrant caution. Best suited for Ahmedabad (home market) and specific Mumbai projects with established delivery history.
