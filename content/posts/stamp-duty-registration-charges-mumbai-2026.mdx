## Mumbai Stamp Duty Rates (2026)

Mumbai follows the Maharashtra Stamp Act. Current rates effective from April 2025:

| Category | Within BMC Limits | Within MMRDA (outside BMC) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Male buyer | 6% | 6% |
| Female buyer (sole/first-named) | 5% | 5% |
| Registration charge | 1% (max Rs 30,000) | 1% (max Rs 30,000) |
| Metro cess | 1% | 1% |
| Local Body Tax | 1% (in some areas) | Nil |
| **Total (male)** | **8-9%** | **7-8%** |
| **Total (female)** | **7-8%** | **6-7%** |

## How to Save on Stamp Duty in Mumbai

### Women Buyer Concession

Register property in a woman's name (wife, mother, daughter) as the sole or first-named purchaser to save 1% stamp duty. On a Rs 1 Cr property, this saves Rs 1 lakh.

### Joint Registration

If buying jointly with spouse, name the female spouse first in the sale deed to claim the concession.

## Online Stamp Duty Payment Process

Maharashtra has fully digitized stamp duty payment through IGR (Inspector General of Registration) portal:

1. Visit igrmaharashtra.gov.in
2. Select "E-Filing" and property type
3. Enter property details and transaction value
4. Pay stamp duty and registration charges online
5. Book Sub-Registrar appointment for registration
6. Complete biometric verification and registration

## Ready Reckoner Value (Circle Rate)

Mumbai uses Annual Statement of Rates (ASR) published by IGR Maharashtra. The Ready Reckoner value is the minimum value for stamp duty calculation. If your purchase price is higher than the Ready Reckoner value, stamp duty is calculated on the purchase price.

## Exemptions and Concessions

- **Slum Rehabilitation Authority (SRA) flats:** Reduced stamp duty
- **PMAY beneficiaries:** State may offer additional concession
- **Agricultural land:** Lower rates apply
- **Gift deed to family:** Stamp duty at concessional rate (Rs 200 in some cases)

## Common Mistakes

1. Calculating stamp duty on agreement value when Ready Reckoner is higher
2. Not claiming women buyer concession
3. Paying stamp duty on carpet area when it should be on total agreement value
4. Not including GST-paid amount in stamp duty calculation for under-construction properties
