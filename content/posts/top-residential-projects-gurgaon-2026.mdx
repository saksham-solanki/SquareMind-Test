## Project Highlights

### DLF The Arbour — Ultra-Premium, Ultra-Liquid

DLF The Arbour is Gurgaon's most sought-after residential project. At ₹18,000-24,000/sqft, it's premium, but DLF's brand ensures resale demand that no other NCR developer can match. Located near Golf Course Road with DLF commercial developments providing employment demand.

**Best for:** HNI investors and NRIs seeking the safest, most liquid NCR investment.

### Sobha City — Bangalore Quality, Gurgaon Location

Sobha City brings Bangalore's construction quality standards to Gurgaon's Dwarka Expressway. At ₹9,000-12,000/sqft, it offers a 30-40% discount to DLF while delivering Sobha's signature build quality. Our top pick for quality-conscious investors at this price point.

### Godrej Meridien — Best Value in Premium Segment

Godrej Meridien on the Dwarka Expressway offers the Godrej brand at ₹10,000-13,000/sqft — reasonable for the corridor. 2BHK starting at ₹75L with Godrej's reliable delivery track record.
