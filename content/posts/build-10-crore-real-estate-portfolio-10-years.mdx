## The Math Behind ₹10 Crore in 10 Years

Building a ₹10 crore real estate portfolio from scratch in 10 years is ambitious but achievable with disciplined execution, leverage, and compound appreciation. The key insight: you don't need ₹10 crore in cash. With 70-80% leverage, you need approximately ₹2-2.5 crore in equity over the decade, invested in properties that collectively appreciate to ₹10 crore.

## The 10-Year Blueprint

### Phase 1 (Year 1-2): Foundation — ₹1.5 Crore in Assets

**Starting capital required:** ₹35-45 lakhs (savings + family support)

| Property | City | Cost | Down Payment | Loan | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2BHK Growth Corridor | Bangalore/Hyderabad | ₹75L | ₹18L | ₹57L | Appreciation |
| 2BHK IT Corridor | Pune/Chennai | ₹50L | ₹12L | ₹38L | Yield |

**End of Year 2 portfolio value:** ₹1.5Cr (cost) → ₹1.7-1.85Cr (with 12% avg appreciation)

### Phase 2 (Year 3-5): Scale — ₹4 Crore in Assets

**Additional capital required:** ₹50-70 lakhs (savings + Property 1/2 appreciation as refinance equity)

| Property | City | Cost | Source of Down Payment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 3BHK Premium Growth | Mumbai (Panvel) / Gurgaon | ₹1.2Cr | Savings + refinance equity from Property 1 |
| 2BHK Yield Optimised | Hyderabad/Pune | ₹55L | Savings + rental income accumulated |

**End of Year 5 portfolio value:** ₹4Cr (cost) → ₹5.5-6.5Cr (with 12% avg appreciation)

### Phase 3 (Year 6-8): Optimise — ₹7.5 Crore in Assets

**Strategy shift:** Sell underperformers from Phase 1, redeploy capital into higher-value assets.

| Action | Property | Capital Unlocked/Deployed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sell | Phase 1 yield property (appreciated 60-80%) | ₹80L-1Cr net proceeds |
| Buy | Commercial office (Bangalore/Mumbai) | ₹1.5Cr for 6-8% yield |
| Buy | Premium residential (Gurgaon/Bangalore) | ₹1.2Cr |

### Phase 4 (Year 9-10): Consolidate — ₹10 Crore Portfolio

By year 9-10, accumulated appreciation across 4-5 properties crosses ₹10 crore total value. Refinance to reduce leverage, shift from growth to income focus.

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Start with growth corridors:** Years 1-5, prioritise appreciation over yield. Sarjapur, Panvel, Hinjewadi Phase 3 are the plays.
2. **Use leverage wisely:** 75-80% LTV in years 1-5, reduce to 50-60% by year 7-8, target 30-40% by year 10.
3. **Refinance to unlock equity:** When a property appreciates 30%+, refinance to extract equity for the next purchase.
4. **Diversify across 3 cities minimum:** Single-city risk is unacceptable at ₹10Cr scale.
5. **Transition to income:** From year 7 onwards, shift new purchases to yield-focused (commercial, established residential).

## The Risk Reality

This strategy requires:
- Stable income to service EMIs on 2-4 simultaneous loans
- Ability to manage negative cash flow in years 1-5 (rental income won't cover EMIs)
- Willingness to sell underperformers (emotional attachment to properties is the #1 portfolio killer)
- Market conditions that deliver 10-14% average appreciation (historical average for growth corridors)
