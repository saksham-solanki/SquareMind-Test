## Why NRIs Are Investing in Bangalore in 2026

Bangalore is the number one destination for NRI real estate investment in India. The IT capital's strong rental demand (driven by a massive tech workforce), upcoming metro expansion (Yellow and Purple line extensions), and globally connected airport make it ideal for NRIs who understand the tech ecosystem.

## Best Areas in Bangalore for NRI Property Investment

### Premium Localities

Top NRI investment areas include Whitefield, Sarjapur Road, Hebbal, and Devanahalli (near airport). These localities offer the highest rental demand from tech professionals.

| Locality | Price Range (per sq ft) | Rental Yield | Key Driver |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Whitefield | Rs 7,000-14,000 | 3.5-4.5% | IT corridor, metro coming |
| Sarjapur Road | Rs 6,500-12,000 | 3.5-4% | Tech parks, schools |
| Hebbal | Rs 8,000-15,000 | 3-3.5% | Airport road, Manyata Tech Park |
| Devanahalli | Rs 5,000-8,000 | 2.5-3% | Airport proximity, growth zone |

### Emerging Investment Zones

Electronic City (Phase 2), Yelahanka, and Kanakapura Road offer 20-35% lower prices with strong appreciation potential as metro connectivity improves.

## Karnataka Stamp Duty and Registration for NRI Buyers

Karnataka charges 5% stamp duty plus 1% registration charge. An additional 2% surcharge applies for properties above Rs 45 lakhs in BBMP limits. No gender-based concession in Karnataka.

## Top Builders in Bangalore for NRI Buyers

Reputable developers include Prestige Group, Sobha Limited, Brigade Group, and Puravankara. Bangalore has the highest RERA compliance among Indian cities. Verify:

1. Karnataka RERA registration on rera.karnataka.gov.in
2. Builder's track record for delivery timelines
3. NRI sales desk with POA and documentation support
4. Bank tie-ups with SBI, HDFC, ICICI for NRI loans

## FEMA Compliance for Bangalore Property

Standard [FEMA rules](/insights/nri-buying-property-india-guide-2026) apply. Bangalore-specific notes:

- Payments through NRE/NRO account or direct foreign remittance
- Revenue sites and agricultural land (common in Bangalore outskirts) require extra due diligence — verify land conversion from agricultural to residential
- POA must be Consulate-attested and registered at Karnataka Sub-Registrar
- Khata certificate is mandatory for property registration in BBMP limits

## NRI Tax Implications for Bangalore Property

Use our [NRI Tax Calculator](/tools/nri-tax-calculator) to estimate tax liability.

- **Rental income:** 30% TDS. Bangalore's strong rental market (3-4.5% yields) means meaningful rental income to report
- **Capital gains:** 12.5% LTCG without indexation. 20% TDS on sale
- **Property tax:** BBMP property tax must be paid annually even by NRI owners

## Property Verification

1. **Title search** and **Khata certificate** verification
2. **Encumbrance certificate** from Karnataka Sub-Registrar (30 years)
3. **RERA registration** on Karnataka RERA portal
4. **Land conversion certificate** (critical for plots outside BBMP limits)
5. **Betterment charges** and **BBMP approval** status
