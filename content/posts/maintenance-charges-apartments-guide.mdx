## What Maintenance Charges Cover

Monthly maintenance fees paid to the housing society or management company cover:

| Category | Includes |
| --- | --- |
| Common area upkeep | Lobby, corridors, garden, parking |
| Security | Guards, CCTV, access control |
| Utilities | Common area electricity, water pump, lift electricity |
| Amenities | Swimming pool, gym, clubhouse maintenance |
| Staff salaries | Housekeeping, plumbers, electricians |
| Sinking fund | Emergency repairs, major maintenance |
| Insurance | Building insurance premium |

## What to Check Before Buying

1. **Current maintenance rate** and history of increases
2. **What is included** — some charge extra for parking, generator, water
3. **Sinking fund contribution** — mandatory for major repairs
4. **Builder maintenance vs society** — rates often increase when society takes over
5. **Maintenance-free period** — some builders offer 12-24 months free
6. **Penalty for non-payment** — understand society rules

## Builder Maintenance vs Society Maintenance

| Phase | Who Manages | Typical Rate | Quality |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pre-society (builder) | Builder's facility team | Often lower (subsidized) | Variable |
| Post-society handover | Resident committee | Market rate (usually increases 30-50%) | Depends on committee |

## Red Flags

1. Maintenance quoted suspiciously low (will increase sharply after possession)
2. No sinking fund provision
3. Builder refusing to share maintenance breakdown
4. Very high amenities but no disclosure of ongoing maintenance cost
5. No clear timeline for society formation
