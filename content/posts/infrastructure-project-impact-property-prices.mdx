## The Infrastructure Premium

Infrastructure development is the single biggest driver of property price appreciation in India. But how much impact does each type of project have? We analysed completed projects to quantify the effect.

## Impact by Infrastructure Type

| Project Type | Price Impact (0-3 km) | Timeline | Data Points |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Metro line | +15-30% | Announcement to 2 yrs post-completion | Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad metros |
| Airport | +20-45% | 3-5 years before opening | Navi Mumbai, Jewar, Devanahalli |
| Expressway | +15-25% | During construction to completion | Dwarka Expressway, ORR Hyderabad |
| IT Park/SEZ | +12-20% | 2-3 years post-occupancy | Whitefield, HITEC City, Hinjewadi |
| Bullet train station | +10-20%* | Still speculative | Mumbai-Ahmedabad corridor |

*Bullet train impact is estimated based on global precedents (Japan, China).

## When to Buy: The Infrastructure Investment Timeline

| Phase | Price Impact | Risk Level | Opportunity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Announcement | +5-10% (immediate) | High (project may be cancelled) | Best entry point |
| Land acquisition | +5-8% | Moderate | Good entry |
| Construction start | +8-12% | Low-Moderate | Still good |
| 50% completion | +5-8% | Low | Moderate gains remaining |
| Completion | +5-10% | Very low | Premium priced in |

**Optimal entry:** After land acquisition, before construction starts. Project cancellation risk is low, and 60-70% of appreciation is still ahead.
