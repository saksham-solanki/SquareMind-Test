## Bangalore Rental Market Overview

Bangalore has India's highest rental demand driven by the IT sector. Post-pandemic return-to-office mandates have pushed rents up 30-50% from 2022 levels. Yields are among the best in Indian metros.

## Key Insight

Bangalore offers 4.0-5.0% gross rental yields in IT corridors — among the best in India. Electronic City provides the highest yield (5.0%) due to lower property prices and strong IT tenant demand. Central Bangalore yields are lower (3.5-3.7%) but offer better capital appreciation.

## Rental Demand Trends

1. **Return-to-office** — IT companies mandating 3-4 days in office drives proximity demand
2. **Startup ecosystem** — Koramangala and HSR Layout see strong demand from startup employees
3. **Furnished premium** — furnished apartments command 30-40% premium rents
4. **Co-living growth** — shared living reducing demand for studio/1BHK rentals
