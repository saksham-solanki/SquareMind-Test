## NCR Rental Yields: The Gurgaon Premium vs Noida Value

Delhi NCR's rental market is sharply divided. Gurgaon commands premium rents but higher capital values compress yields. Noida and Greater Noida offer better yield math — lower purchase prices with reasonable rents from IT professionals.

## Greater Noida West: NCR's Yield Champion

Greater Noida West delivers NCR's highest yields because capital values (₹3,500-5,000/sqft) are among the region's lowest while rental demand from Noida IT corridor employees and university students is consistent.

**Key advantage:** A 2BHK costing ₹25-38L can rent for ₹9,000-14,000/month — yielding 3.4-4.0% gross.

**Risk:** Builder quality varies significantly. Only buy delivered units from builders with proven track records (Gaursons, ATS, Ace).

### New Gurgaon: Balanced Yield-Appreciation

Sectors 76-95 in New Gurgaon offer the best balance of rental yield (3.0-3.4%) and appreciation potential. Corporate tenants from nearby IT parks (DLF Cyber City, Udyog Vihar) provide reliable demand.

## NCR-Specific Considerations

- **Maintenance charges:** NCR societies charge ₹4-8/sqft maintenance (₹2,000-5,000/month for a 2BHK), significantly impacting net yield
- **Property tax:** Varies by state — Haryana (Gurgaon) has higher property tax than UP (Noida)
- **Lease registration:** Mandatory in Haryana for leases over 11 months, optional in UP

## The Bottom Line

NCR's best rental yields are in Greater Noida West (3.4-4.0%) and New Gurgaon sectors (3.0-3.4%). For pure yield, Greater Noida offers better numbers. For yield + liquidity + appreciation, New Gurgaon is the safer bet. Avoid premium Gurgaon corridors for rental income — yields below 2.5% don't justify the capital locked up.
