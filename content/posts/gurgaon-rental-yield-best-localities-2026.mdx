## Gurgaon Rental Yields: Corporate Demand Meets Premium Pricing

Gurgaon has India's highest absolute rents outside Mumbai premium — a 2BHK on Golf Course Road rents for ₹40,000-65,000/month. But high capital values compress yields. The yield sweet spot is in New Gurgaon and Sector 37D, where moderate pricing meets strong corporate rental demand.

## Top Yield Picks

### Sector 37D: Gurgaon's Hidden Yield Gem

Sector 37D is often overlooked because it lacks the marketing glitz of Dwarka Expressway or Golf Course Extension. But at ₹6,000-8,000/sqft with proximity to Udyog Vihar and old Gurgaon's commercial district, it delivers yields of 2.8-3.4% — among Gurgaon's best.

**Key advantage:** Established micro-market with operational social infrastructure, reducing vacancy risk.

### New Gurgaon: Yield + Growth

Sectors 81-89 in New Gurgaon balance yield (2.8-3.2%) with appreciation potential (14% CAGR). Corporate tenants from expanding offices along NH-8 and Sohna Road provide steady demand.

Gurgaon's best rental yields are in Sector 37D and New Gurgaon — not in the premium corridors that generate the most media attention. If rental income is your priority, avoid Golf Course Road and premium Dwarka Expressway. The yield math works best at ₹6,000-9,000/sqft entry points.
