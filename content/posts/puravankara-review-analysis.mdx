## Independent Analysis

Puravankara is one of South India's oldest listed developers with 50+ years of history. This analysis is based on RERA data, financial filings, and buyer feedback.

## Company Overview

- **Listed company:** NSE: PURVA | BSE: 532891
- **Headquarters:** Bangalore
- **Founded:** 1975
- **Brands:** Puravankara (premium), Provident Housing (affordable)
- **Active cities:** Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Kochi
- **Revenue (FY2025):** ₹3,840 crore
- **Market Cap:** ~₹14,000 crore

## RERA Delivery Track Record

Analysis of 30 Puravankara and Provident projects under Karnataka and other state RERAs (2017-2023).

| Category | Projects | % of Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Delivered on time | 12 | 40.0% |
| Delayed 6–18 months | 10 | 33.3% |
| Delayed 18–36 months | 6 | 20.0% |
| Significantly delayed | 2 | 6.7% |

**Assessment:** 40% on-time delivery is above industry average. Provident Housing (affordable arm) has slightly better delivery consistency than the premium Puravankara brand, likely due to simpler specifications.

## Dual Brand Strategy

**Puravankara (Premium):** ₹7,000-15,000/sqft range, larger apartments, premium finishes
**Provident Housing (Affordable):** ₹3,500-6,000/sqft range, compact units, value-focused

This strategy is clever — it captures both segments without diluting brand positioning. Provident has actually been the stronger performer in recent years.

## Our Verdict

**Overall Grade: B**

Puravankara is a solid South Indian developer with good financial health and a smart dual-brand strategy. Not best-in-class for delivery, but reliable. The Provident Housing brand offers genuinely good value in the affordable segment.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Puravankara better than Prestige?

Prestige has better delivery rates (50% vs 40%) and stronger brand recognition. Puravankara offers the advantage of an affordable brand (Provident) and competitive pricing in the premium segment.

### Is Provident Housing reliable?

Provident Housing by Puravankara is one of India's better affordable housing brands. Build quality is functional, not luxury, but delivery consistency is good and pricing is genuine value-for-money.

### Where should I buy Puravankara?

Bangalore is their strongest market. Henley Road, Whitefield, and Sarjapur Road projects have good track records. Chennai is also reliable. Be cautious with newer markets.
