## The NRI Landlord's Dilemma: How to Collect Rent From 10,000 Miles Away

Owning property in India while living abroad creates a unique management challenge. Tenants default, maintenance issues arise, and TDS compliance falls on you. Here's a practical framework for remote property management.

## Property Management Options

| Option | Cost | Control | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Family member | Free | Variable | Single property, trusted relative nearby |
| Professional PM company | 8-12% of rent | Limited | Multiple properties, hands-off approach |
| Individual manager | 5-8% of rent | Moderate | Single city, personal relationship |
| Self-manage (remote) | Free | Full | Tech-savvy NRIs, short-term |

## Professional Property Management: What You Get

A good PM company handles:
- Tenant screening and selection
- Rent collection and deposit into NRO account
- Maintenance coordination
- Society meeting representation
- Property inspection (quarterly)
- Tenant eviction if needed
- TDS compliance on rent

## TDS on NRI Rental Income

Tenants paying rent to NRIs must deduct TDS:

| Scenario | TDS Rate | Deducted By |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tenant is individual | 30% + surcharge + cess | Tenant (must obtain TAN) |
| Tenant is company | 30% + surcharge + cess | Company (existing TAN) |
| PM company collects rent | 30% on PM's remittance to NRI | PM company |

**Issue:** Individual tenants often refuse NRI landlords because they don't want TAN registration responsibility. Solution: route rent through PM company.

### Reducing TDS
Apply for lower TDS certificate (Form 13) from Income Tax department. Actual tax liability is usually lower than 30% after standard deduction and exemptions.

## NRO Account Setup for Rent

All Indian rental income must flow through NRO account:
1. Open NRO savings account with major bank (SBI, HDFC, ICICI)
2. Set up automatic standing instruction for society maintenance deductions
3. Track rental income deposits monthly
4. Repatriate excess funds to overseas account (up to USD 1M/year via LRS)

## Remote Monitoring Checklist

| Task | Frequency | Method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rent deposit verification | Monthly | NRO account statement |
| Property condition check | Quarterly | PM report + photos/video call |
| Society dues payment | Monthly | Auto-debit from NRO |
| Property tax payment | Annual | Online (most municipalities) |
| Tenant lease renewal | Annual | Digital signature or POA |
| Insurance renewal | Annual | Online |

## Common Problems and Solutions

1. **Tenant stops paying:** PM company handles notices and eviction. Without PM, you need a local lawyer
2. **Major repair needed:** PM authorizes up to pre-agreed limit. Above limit, NRI approves via video call
3. **Tenant damages property:** Security deposit covers. PM documents damage with photos
4. **Society issues:** PM attends meetings as authorized representative via POA

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it worth owning rental property in India as an NRI?

If net yield (after PM fees, TDS, maintenance, vacancy) exceeds 3%, yes. Properties in IT corridors of Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Pune typically deliver 3.5-4.5% net yield for NRI landlords. Below 3% net, you're better off investing in Indian mutual funds.

### Can I evict a tenant remotely?

Not directly. You need either a PM company or a local authorized person (via POA) to file eviction proceedings. The process takes 3-12 months depending on the city and type of agreement.

### Should I furnish my Indian property for better rent?

Yes. Furnished properties command 25-35% higher rent in IT cities. Budget Rs 3-5 lakhs for quality furnishing. The investment is recovered in 12-18 months through higher rent.
