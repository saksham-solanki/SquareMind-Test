## What to Verify Before Buying

1. **HMDA/DTCP approval:** Ensure the layout has Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority or DTCP approval
2. **FTL zone check:** Post-HYDRAA, properties near lakes must be verified for Full Tank Level zone clearance
3. **ORR access distance:** Properties more than 5 km from an ORR junction face connectivity challenges
4. **Water connection:** Confirm HMWSSB connection — borewell-dependent projects face long-term risk
