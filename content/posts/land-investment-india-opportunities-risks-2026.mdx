## Why Land Is India's Highest-Return (and Highest-Risk) Real Estate Asset

Land in India has historically delivered 15-25% CAGR in growth corridors — significantly outperforming apartments. The reason is simple: land appreciates based on development potential, not just rental income. A plot near an upcoming metro station, airport, or highway can multiply in value as infrastructure develops.

But land investment also carries India's highest real estate risks: title disputes, land-use restrictions, encroachment, regulatory changes, and zero rental income during the holding period.

## Critical Legal Checks for Land Purchase

1. **Title verification:** 30-year title search through a property lawyer. Non-negotiable.
2. **Land use classification:** NA (non-agricultural) conversion is required for residential development. Agricultural land has purchase restrictions.
3. **Encumbrance certificate:** Verify no existing loans, liens, or legal claims on the land.
4. **Survey number and mutation records:** Match revenue department records with the seller's documents.
5. **Zoning regulations:** Confirm the plot falls within residential/commercial zoning, not green belt or industrial.
6. **RERA/development authority approval:** Plots in approved layouts (BDA, HMDA, GMADA, HUDA) are significantly safer than revenue plots.

## Agricultural Land: The Special Case

Agricultural land investment is restricted by state laws:
- Most states prohibit non-agriculturists from buying agricultural land
- Conversion to non-agricultural (NA) use requires government approval (6-24 months)
- Returns can be exceptional (20-30% CAGR) near urbanising areas, but legal complexity is high

**Our recommendation:** Unless you have local expertise and legal counsel, avoid agricultural land. The risk-reward is only justified for experienced investors.

## The Bottom Line

Land investment in India offers the highest appreciation potential but demands the most due diligence. Focus on approved layout plots (BDA, HMDA, GMADA) in infrastructure-catalysed corridors. Budget for legal verification costs (₹25,000-50,000) and accept zero rental income. If you can't verify title independently, this asset class is not for you.
