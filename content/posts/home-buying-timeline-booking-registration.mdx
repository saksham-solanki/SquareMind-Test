## The Home Buying Timeline: 6 Key Stages

Most first-time buyers are surprised by how many steps and how much time the buying process takes. Here is the realistic timeline.

## Stage 1: Token/Booking Amount (Day 1)

| Detail | Information |
| --- | --- |
| Amount | 1-5% of property value (Rs 50K-5L typically) |
| Purpose | Shows serious intent, holds the unit |
| Refundable? | Partially — depends on agreement terms |
| Document | Booking receipt/allotment letter |
| Timeline | Same day as decision |

**Important:** Get a proper receipt. Clarify refund terms in writing before paying.

## Stage 2: Home Loan Sanction (Week 1-4)

- Submit property documents to bank
- Bank conducts legal and technical verification
- Sanction letter issued with approved amount and rate
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks

## Stage 3: Agreement to Sell (Week 3-6)

| Detail | Information |
| --- | --- |
| What it covers | Price, payment schedule, possession date, specifications |
| Stamp duty | Applicable (full or partial depending on state) |
| Legal review | Mandatory — hire independent lawyer |
| Key clauses | Penalty for delay, quality specifications, cancellation terms |

## Stage 4: Payment Schedule (Week 4 onwards)

**New project (construction-linked):**
- Booking: 10%
- Foundation: 10%
- Plinth: 10%
- Each slab: 5-10%
- Finishing: 10%
- Possession: 5%

**Resale/Ready-to-move:** 90% at agreement, 10% at registration (or as negotiated).

## Stage 5: Stamp Duty and Registration (Week 6-10)

| Step | Timeline |
| --- | --- |
| Calculate stamp duty | Use our [Stamp Duty Calculator](/tools/stamp-duty-calculator) |
| Pay stamp duty online | 1 day |
| Book Sub-Registrar appointment | 1-7 days |
| Registration at SRO | 1 day (both parties present) |
| Receive registered deed | Same day to 7 days |

## Stage 6: Possession (Immediately or 1-4 years)

**Ready-to-move:** Possession within 1-4 weeks of registration.
**Under-construction:** As per RERA-registered timeline.

## Total Timeline Summary

| Type | Total Time (Booking to Moving In) |
| --- | --- |
| Ready-to-move (resale) | 6-10 weeks |
| Ready-to-move (new) | 4-8 weeks |
| Under-construction | 1-4 years |
