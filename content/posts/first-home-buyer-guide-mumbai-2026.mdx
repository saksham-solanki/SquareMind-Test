## Buying Your First Home in Mumbai: A Reality Check

Mumbai is India's most expensive property market. But first-time buyers have more options than they think — if they look beyond South Mumbai and established western suburbs. This guide covers realistic options for first-time buyers in Mumbai in 2026.

## Budget-Friendly Areas for First-Time Buyers

| Area | Price Range (2BHK) | Commute to BKC | Key Advantages |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Thane (Ghodbunder Road) | Rs 50L-85L | 45-60 min | Green cover, metro coming, schools |
| Navi Mumbai (Kharghar) | Rs 55L-90L | 40-55 min | Planned city, airport proximity |
| Virar-Vasai | Rs 30L-55L | 60-80 min | Most affordable, Western Line |
| Panvel | Rs 40L-70L | 50-65 min | Navi Mumbai Airport zone |
| Dombivli | Rs 35L-60L | 50-60 min | Central Line, affordable |
| Mira Road | Rs 40L-65L | 40-50 min | Western Line, improving infra |

## PMAY Eligibility in Mumbai

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana benefits for Mumbai first-time buyers:

| Income Category | Annual Income | Subsidy | Max Loan |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| EWS | Up to Rs 3L | 6.5% on Rs 6L for 20 years | Rs 6L |
| LIG | Rs 3L-6L | 6.5% on Rs 6L for 20 years | Rs 6L |
| MIG-I | Rs 6L-12L | 4% on Rs 9L for 20 years | Rs 9L |
| MIG-II | Rs 12L-18L | 3% on Rs 12L for 20 years | Rs 12L |

**Key condition:** You (or your spouse) must not own a pucca house anywhere in India.

## Stamp Duty Savings

Register in a woman's name (sole or first-named) to save 1% stamp duty. On a Rs 70L property, this saves Rs 70,000. Use our [Stamp Duty Calculator](/tools/stamp-duty-calculator) for exact calculations.

## Step-by-Step Buying Process

1. **Assess finances:** Calculate affordability with [Buy vs Rent Calculator](/tools/buy-vs-rent)
2. **Get pre-approved:** Apply to 2-3 banks simultaneously
3. **Shortlist areas:** Based on budget and commute tolerance
4. **Visit properties:** At least 5-8 options before deciding
5. **Verify documents:** RERA registration on MahaRERA portal, title search
6. **Negotiate:** 5-10% negotiation is normal in Mumbai
7. **Book and register:** Pay token, sign agreement, register at Sub-Registrar

## Common First-Time Buyer Mistakes in Mumbai

1. Stretching EMI beyond 40% of take-home salary
2. Ignoring commute time for cheaper areas
3. Not checking builder track record on MahaRERA
4. Skipping legal title verification to save Rs 15K lawyer fee
5. Not accounting for maintenance charges (Rs 3-8/sq ft/month in Mumbai)
