## Bangalore: India's IT Capital Property Map

Bangalore has overtaken NCR as India's largest residential real estate market by volume. IT sector growth, startup ecosystem, and infrastructure development drive sustained demand.

Bangalore property has appreciated 35-50% from 2020 lows. Best current value lies in North Bangalore (Devanahalli, Yelahanka) and select East Bangalore corridors (Varthur, Electronic City). Central Bangalore is priced out for investment; good for end-use only.
