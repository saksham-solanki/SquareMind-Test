## Mumbai Rental Market Overview

Mumbai has the highest absolute rents in India but some of the lowest rental yields. The disconnect between property prices and rents creates an unusual market where owning is often more expensive than renting.

## Key Insight

Mumbai's average gross rental yield is 3.0-3.5% — among the lowest in India. Extended MMR offers marginally better yields (3.7-3.8%). For pure rental income, Mumbai is not the best market. The investment thesis rests on capital appreciation.

## Rental Demand Drivers

1. **IT/Finance sector** — Powai, Goregaon, BKC catchment
2. **Migration** — continuous inflow keeps vacancy low
3. **Corporate housing** — MNC relocations drive premium rentals
4. **Redevelopment displacement** — temporary rental demand in South and Central Mumbai
