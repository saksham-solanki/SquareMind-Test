## From Office Parks to Homes

Embassy Group is best known for Embassy Office Parks REIT — India's first listed REIT. Their residential arm is growing but remains secondary to their commercial dominance.

## Company Overview

- **Private company** (Embassy Office Parks REIT is listed separately)
- **Headquarters:** Bangalore
- **Founded:** 1993 by Jitu Virwani
- **Commercial portfolio:** 50+ million sqft of office space
- **Residential:** Growing presence in Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad
- **Embassy REIT:** NSE: EMBASSY (market cap ~₹33,000 crore)

## Residential RERA Track Record

Analysis of 10 Embassy residential projects under Karnataka RERA (2017-2023).

| Category | Projects | % of Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Delivered on time | 4 | 40.0% |
| Delayed 6–18 months | 4 | 40.0% |
| Delayed 18–36 months | 2 | 20.0% |
| Significantly delayed | 0 | 0% |

**Assessment:** 40% on-time delivery is above average. No significantly delayed projects. Embassy's commercial execution discipline partially transfers to residential, but delays are more common than in their office projects.

## The Commercial Advantage

Embassy's residential projects near their office parks benefit from:

- **Built-in demand** — IT professionals working in Embassy parks want to live nearby
- **Infrastructure** — roads, utilities already developed around commercial parks
- **Maintenance** — Embassy's FM team manages residential communities well

## Our Verdict

**Overall Grade: B**

Strong commercial developer with a growing residential portfolio. Embassy's strength lies in projects near their own office parks. For other locations, the value proposition is less clear.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Embassy Group good for residential projects?

Embassy's residential projects near their office parks are well-executed. Standalone residential projects have less differentiation. Focus on projects with the Embassy ecosystem advantage.

### Is Embassy Group financially stable?

The REIT provides stable annuity income. The private group's finances are less transparent but appear solid. Embassy Office Parks REIT is one of Asia's largest REITs.
