## Chandigarh Tri-City: India's Best-Kept Real Estate Secret

The Chandigarh Tri-City — Chandigarh, Mohali (SAS Nagar), and Panchkula — has quietly delivered 10-14% annual appreciation in select corridors since 2021. The region benefits from three factors most investors overlook: highest per capita income among tier-2 cities, IT City Mohali creating tech employment, and NRI diaspora demand from Punjab and Haryana.

The extended Tri-City includes Zirakpur, New Chandigarh (Mullanpur), and Aerocity — adding affordable growth corridors to the mix.

## Mohali IT City: The Top Pick

Mohali's IT City (Knowledge City) is the region's primary growth catalyst. Infosys, Quark, and multiple IT companies have campuses here, creating a professional tenant pool similar to Hinjewadi or Electronic City at a fraction of the cost.

At ₹5,500-8,000/sqft, you get a 3BHK apartment for ₹55-80L — a configuration that costs ₹1.2-1.8Cr in Bangalore or Hyderabad IT corridors.

**Key builders:** Motiaz, ATS, Emaar (Mohali Hills), and Ambience have projects.

### Zirakpur: The Affordable Gateway

Zirakpur connects to Chandigarh via the Ambala-Chandigarh highway and has emerged as the region's most affordable growth market. At ₹4,500-6,500/sqft, it offers the lowest entry point in the Tri-City from known developers.

**Best for:** Budget investors (₹25-45L) seeking rental income from Chandigarh city workforce.

## NRI Investment Angle

The Tri-City receives significant NRI investment from the Punjabi and Haryanvi diaspora in Canada, UK, and Australia. This creates premium demand for plotted developments and independent floors — a format rare in other Indian metros.

## The Bottom Line

Chandigarh Tri-City offers India's best value proposition in the ₹25-60L range — quality of life comparable to tier-1 cities at tier-2 pricing. Mohali IT City is the standout for IT-linked investment, while Zirakpur serves budget investors. The NRI demand floor provides additional safety.
