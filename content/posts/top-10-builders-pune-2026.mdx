## Pune: India's Most Active Real Estate Market

Pune has the highest number of RERA-registered projects in India. This means more choices but also more due diligence required. Here are the top 10 builders based on data.

## Pune-Specific RERA Note

MahaRERA is India's most active RERA authority. Check MahaRERA.maharashtra.gov.in for any Pune project. Look for: QR code on marketing material (mandatory), registered carpet area, and possession date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which is the best builder in Pune?

For construction quality: Sobha. For transparency: Godrej (listed). For local expertise: Kolte Patil. For value: VTP Realty. The "best" depends on your priority.

### Is Pune better than Mumbai for real estate investment?

Pune offers 40-60% lower prices than Mumbai with strong IT-driven demand. Rental yields are similar. Pune is better for entry-level investors; Mumbai for liquidity and premium appreciation.
