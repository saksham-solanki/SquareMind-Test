## Delhi Stamp Duty Rates (2026)

| Property Value | Male Buyer | Female Buyer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Up to Rs 25 lakh | 4% | 2% |
| Rs 25 lakh to Rs 50 lakh | 5% | 3% |
| Above Rs 50 lakh | 6% | 4% |
| Registration charge | 1% | 1% |

Delhi offers one of the most generous women buyer concessions in India — 2% lower across all slabs.

## How to Calculate Total Cost

For a Rs 1 Cr property with a male buyer:
- Stamp duty: Rs 6,00,000 (6%)
- Registration: Rs 1,00,000 (1%)
- **Total: Rs 7,00,000 (7%)**

With female buyer:
- Stamp duty: Rs 4,00,000 (4%)
- Registration: Rs 1,00,000 (1%)
- **Total: Rs 5,00,000 (5%)**

**Savings with women buyer: Rs 2,00,000**

## Circle Rate in Delhi

Delhi Government publishes circle rates area-wise. Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: actual transaction value or circle rate. Circle rates are categorized by colony type (A to H):

| Category | Example Colonies | Circle Rate Range (per sq mt) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A | Golf Links, Jor Bagh | Rs 7,70,000+ |
| B | Defence Colony, GK-1 | Rs 3,00,000-5,00,000 |
| C | Safdarjung Enclave | Rs 1,50,000-3,00,000 |
| D-E | Dwarka, Vasant Kunj | Rs 70,000-1,50,000 |
| F-H | Outer Delhi | Rs 25,000-70,000 |

## DDA Property Special Rules

DDA (Delhi Development Authority) flats have specific stamp duty rules:
- Stamp duty on DDA allotment is calculated on allotment price
- Subsequent resale: stamp duty on current circle rate or transaction value (higher)
- Conversion from leasehold to freehold involves additional charges

## Online Registration Process

1. Visit doris.delhigovt.nic.in (Delhi Registration portal)
2. Register and create account
3. Enter property and buyer details
4. Pay stamp duty and registration fees online
5. Book Sub-Registrar appointment
6. Visit with documents for biometric registration
