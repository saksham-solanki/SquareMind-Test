## NCR: Buyer Beware Market

Delhi NCR has the worst builder track record among Indian metros. The region has seen multiple abandoned projects, builder defaults, and buyer protests. Choosing the right builder here is critical.

## Delhi NCR Builder Rankings 2026

On-time delivery: 37% | Near-zero debt | Gurgaon's dominant developer with 75+ years. Premium pricing but strong resale market. Best in established Gurgaon sectors.

## NCR-Specific Warning

NCR has the highest rate of project delays and defaults in India. **Critical checks before buying:**

1. Verify builder has no pending cases on consumer forums
2. Check Haryana RERA or UP RERA registration (depending on location)
3. Visit actual site — photos can be misleading
4. Check if builder has delivered at least 3 previous projects on time

Verify on [RERA Verification Tool](/tools/rera-verifier). Assess with [Investment Scorecard](/tools/investment-scorecard).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which is safer — Gurgaon or Noida for buying property?

Gurgaon has better builder quality (DLF, Tata, Sobha) and infrastructure. Noida offers lower prices but higher builder risk. Sector-by-sector analysis matters more than city-level comparison.

### Should I avoid NCR builders entirely?

No — but stick to listed companies or established conglomerate-backed developers. Avoid unlisted mid-size developers with limited delivery history.
