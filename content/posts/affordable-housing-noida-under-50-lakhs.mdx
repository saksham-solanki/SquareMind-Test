## Critical Rule: Buy Only Delivered

In Noida's affordable segment, the golden rule is: **never buy under-construction from a builder without 90%+ delivery track record.** The NCLT cases of Amrapali, Jaypee, and Unitech are cautionary tales. In 2026, enough delivered inventory exists that you never need to take construction risk.

A delivered 2BHK in Greater Noida West at ₹30-42L from Gaursons or ATS, with 70%+ building occupancy, delivers 3.2-4.0% rental yield and positions for Jewar Airport appreciation. Walk through the building before buying.
