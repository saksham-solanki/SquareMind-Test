## Gurgaon: Corporate Hub with Premium Aspirations

Gurgaon is where corporate India lives. First-time buyers face a challenge: premium sector pricing vs emerging corridor affordability. The Dwarka Expressway has opened new affordable options.

## Women Buyer Advantage

Haryana offers 2% stamp duty concession for women. On a Rs 60L flat:
- Male: Rs 4.7L (7% + Rs 50K)
- Female: Rs 3.5L (5% + Rs 50K)
- **Savings: Rs 1.2L**

## Gurgaon-Specific Caution

1. **Builder track record:** Gurgaon has mixed builder reliability — check H-RERA for delivery history
2. **Colony license:** Verify project has valid colony license from DTCP
3. **EDC/IDC dues:** Some projects have pending External Development Charges — this becomes buyer's liability
4. **Occupation Certificate:** Many projects face OC delays — verify status
