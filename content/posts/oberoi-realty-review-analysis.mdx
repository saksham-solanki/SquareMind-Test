## Mumbai's Premium Developer

Oberoi Realty operates almost exclusively in Mumbai's premium segment. This review examines whether their focused approach delivers better outcomes than diversified peers.

## Company Overview

- **Listed company:** NSE: OBEROIRLTY | BSE: 533273
- **Headquarters:** Mumbai
- **Founded:** 1998 by Vikas Oberoi
- **Focus:** Luxury and premium residential in Mumbai
- **Active areas:** Goregaon (Oberoi Garden City), Worli, Borivali, Thane
- **Revenue (FY2025):** ₹4,680 crore
- **Market Cap:** ~₹58,000 crore

## RERA Delivery Track Record

Analysis of 16 Oberoi Realty projects under MahaRERA (2017-2023).

| Category | Projects | % of Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Delivered on time | 10 | 62.5% |
| Delayed 6–18 months | 4 | 25.0% |
| Delayed 18–36 months | 2 | 12.5% |
| Significantly delayed | 0 | 0% |

**Assessment:** 62.5% on-time delivery with zero significantly delayed projects is exceptional. Oberoi's focused strategy — few projects, single city, premium segment — enables this consistency.

## Financial Health

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Assessment |

| Revenue | ₹4,680 Cr | ₹3,920 Cr | +19.4% growth |
| EBITDA Margin | 42.8% | 40.2% | Industry-leading |
| Net Debt | ₹1,200 Cr | ₹1,800 Cr | Low and declining |
| Debt-to-Equity | 0.15x | 0.24x | Almost debt-free |
| Pre-sales | ₹6,800 Cr | ₹5,400 Cr | Strong |

**Assessment:** Among the most financially healthy developers in India. 42.8% EBITDA margin reflects luxury pricing power. Near-zero debt. This is a financially fortress-like developer.

## Construction Quality

Oberoi Realty's construction quality is among the best in Mumbai:

- **Sky City, Oberoi Garden City** — large-scale township with excellent amenities
- **Three Sixty West (Worli)** — ultra-luxury with Ritz-Carlton branded residences
- **Finish quality** — imported marble, premium fittings, excellent common areas
- **Maintenance** — professional, though expensive

## Our Verdict

**Overall Grade: A**

Oberoi Realty is Mumbai's gold standard for premium residential. Best delivery track record (62.5% on-time), strongest financials, and highest construction quality in the city. Limited by Mumbai-only presence and luxury-only pricing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Oberoi Realty the best builder in Mumbai?

For luxury and premium segments, Oberoi Realty has the strongest combination of delivery consistency, financial health, and construction quality in Mumbai.

### Is Oberoi Garden City Goregaon a good investment?

Oberoi Garden City has shown strong appreciation over 15+ years and remains one of Mumbai's most well-maintained townships. Mature, well-connected, and still appreciating.

### Why is Oberoi Realty so expensive per square foot?

Premium location (Western suburbs, Worli), luxury specifications, and a brand that commands the highest resale premium in Mumbai. The pricing reflects genuine quality differentiation.
