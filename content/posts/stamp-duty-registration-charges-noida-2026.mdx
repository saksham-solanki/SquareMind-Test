## Noida Stamp Duty Rates (2026)

| Category | Rate |
| --- | --- |
| Male buyer | 7% |
| Female buyer (sole) | 5% |
| Registration charge | 1% |
| **Total (male)** | **8%** |
| **Total (female)** | **6%** |

UP offers a 2% women buyer concession. Registration in a woman's name on a Rs 70L property saves Rs 1.4L.

## Leasehold vs Freehold

Most Noida properties are leasehold from Noida Authority / GNIDA. Important implications:

| Factor | Leasehold | Freehold |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Stamp duty basis | On lease premium + transfer fee | On sale value |
| Transfer fee to Authority | 2.5% of current allotment rate | None |
| Lease renewal | Every 30/90 years | Not applicable |
| Bank loan | Available (most banks accept) | Available |

## Registration Process

1. Visit igrsup.gov.in (UP e-Registration)
2. Create account and link Aadhaar
3. Enter property details
4. Pay stamp duty online via e-Stamping
5. Book SRO appointment
6. Complete biometric registration

## Noida-Specific Tips

1. Factor in Noida Authority transfer fee (2.5%) in addition to stamp duty
2. Verify leasehold status and years remaining on lease
3. Check for pending dues to Noida Authority before purchase
4. Women buyer concession provides significant savings
