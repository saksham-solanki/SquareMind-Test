## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Ahmedabad real estate undervalued?

Relative to Pune and Bangalore, Ahmedabad offers lower prices with comparable IT sector growth. GIFT City development could be a long-term price driver. Current pricing represents good value.

### Which area in Ahmedabad is best for investment?

SG Highway for established appreciation, South Bopal for value, Gandhinagar corridor for GIFT City proximity. Prices range from ₹3,500 to ₹8,500/sqft.
