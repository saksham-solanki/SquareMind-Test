    **NRITaxCalc.tsx (TOOL-06):**
    - "use client" component
    - Import `NRI_TAX_PROFILES`, `LTCG_RATE`, `CESS_RATE` from `@/data/nri-tax-data`
    - Two calculation modes (tabs or radio toggle): "Rental Income Tax" and "Capital Gains Tax"

    **Rental Income mode:**
    - Inputs: Country of Residence (select from NRI_TAX_PROFILES), Annual Rental Income in India (Rs.), Municipal Taxes Paid (Rs.)
    - Calculation:
      1. Net rental income = Gross rent - Municipal taxes - 30% standard deduction (Section 24)
      2. TDS rate = profile.tdsOnRental % (or 31.2% if no DTAA)
      3. TDS amount = Net rental income * TDS rate / 100
      4. Cess = TDS * 4/100
      5. Total tax = TDS + Cess
    - Show: Gross rent, deductions breakdown, TDS rate (mention if DTAA rate), tax amount, cess, total

    **Capital Gains mode:**
    - Inputs: Country of Residence, Sale Price (Rs.), Purchase Price (Rs.), Holding Period (years), Improvement Costs (Rs., default 0)
    - Calculation:
      1. If holding > 2 years: LTCG. Capital gain = sale - purchase - improvements. Tax = gain * 12.5%. TDS at source = sale * 12.5%
      2. If holding <= 2 years: STCG. Capital gain = sale - purchase - improvements. Tax = gain * 30% (simplified -- note actual rate depends on total income). TDS at source = sale * 30%
      3. Add 4% cess on tax
      4. Add surcharge if gain > Rs.50L (10% for 50L-1Cr, 15% for 1-2Cr, 25% for 2-5Cr)
    - Show: Capital gain amount, LTCG vs STCG classification, base tax, surcharge (if any), cess, total estimated tax, TDS at source
    - Show country-specific notes from the profile

    **Both modes:** Add prominent disclaimer: "This is an estimate for educational purposes. Tax laws are complex and change frequently. Please consult a Chartered Accountant for exact calculations." Style as a subtle info box at the bottom.
